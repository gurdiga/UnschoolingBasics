Our dd loved her leash as well. She has always been into animals. When she
was 2 she could make tons of animal sounds and did so frequently. I would
use a leash if I had to go to the mall or somewhere the possibility of her
getting away from me could happen. I lost her once in Dillards (a large
department store). In a blink she darted off and decided to play hide &
seek in the clothes rack. Nearly gave me a heart attack. I did not have
the choice of waiting until my DH got home to do my shopping nor did I have
family near to help out. My dh was in graduate school and working 2 jobs
for me to be able to stay home with our dd. So I did what I had to do.
However, all that to say...everytime I would put the leash on her (it was
the harness type) she would immediately start barking like a dog! People
passing by got a real kick out of it and some gave me the "look" as if I
wasn't a very good mom. We made it a game and had fun with it. I guess it
is all in how you treat it. By the time our younger dd came along I had
help and never had to use a leash with her.
