As I have mentioned before, we pretend like there is a tooth fairy, but everyone in my house know it's just a game for us.

My 9 year old lost a tooth this week and we had this conversation;

DD, "Is the tooth fairy going to come visit?"
Me, "I don't know, do you want the tooth fairy to come?"
DD, "Well, I would rather the tooth fairy just take me to the dollar store to buy something."

So off we went to the dollar store!
