Hello,

We recently went through the rather sudden death of my father, who (along
with my mother) had recently moved to our town and built a little house in
our back yard this past year. One book that we liked that was given to us
by friends is called "The Fall of Freddie the Leaf" by Leo Buscaglia.
Another older one that I really like is called "About Dying: An open
family book for parents and children together" by Sara Bonnett Stein --
though it was published in 1974 and might be harder to acquire.

Hope this helps, sorry for the loss of your daughter's grandfather.

Carolyn Winkler
