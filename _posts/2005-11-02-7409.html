Super Nanny will be demonstrating Pivotal Response Treatment this
Fiday. This is a scientifically proven treatment that was born out of
ABA. The book comes out tomorrow. It is used to aquire language as
well as learning new skills and behaviors. It's a very positive,
child centered program. I just went to the semenar today and learned
about the show and the book. UC Santa Barbara is where the research
team is located. Their next project is to compare PECS with PRT to
see how they differ.
