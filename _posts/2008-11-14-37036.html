14 Nov 2008, at 11:29am

Hi everyone!



We are excited to announce that early bird registration for Great Big Happy
Life- An Unschoolers' Conference Retreat will open tomorrow 11/15/08.
Register from 11/15-12/15 and receive 20% off your registration.



Our website is fully up and running and describes lodging, meals program and
presenters. As with so many other unschooling conferences that have taken
root in the past few years, this one was inspired by the Live and Learn
Conference that just had its final magnificent run in September. Great Big
Happy Life will be a blend of inspirational presentations, awesome
participant led funshops, informal discussions, intense relaxation and just
generally hanging out with other whole life/radical unschooling families.
There will be fun stuff for families, great opportunities for teens to hang
and connect, and things to discover for grown-ups and little ones too! We
are constantly updating our program, which right now includes speakers Dayna
Martin and Amy Childs. We are getting great feedback from folks about the
site- and are busy adding events, experiences and presenters to the site! We
hope you can join us in April!



If you want to contact me, email me directly at pnkn314@.... You can
also join our yahoo group to meet others who will be attending, ask
questions, or suggest ideas- http://groups.yahoo.com/group/NJUC/.



We welcome your ideas, questions, suggestions-please post to the list or
email me. We looking forward to seeing you there!



-Jennifer Johnson

Coordinator
