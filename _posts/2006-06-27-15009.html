For spellcheck, download the Google toolbar. I LOVE my Google
toolbar. It has a Google search part, a popup blocker and spellcheck.
I also like that when I google something and go to a webpage that was
in my search I can highlight the words that I was googling so I can
find it easier.

Recently Yahoogroups made a change in the way that messages are sent
out. I had the same problem and found that I needed to go to
yahoogroups, sign in, and go to each individual group I am in and
click "edit membership" Scroll to the bottom and check "traditional"
and save changes. This will stop the messages from coming in "rich
formatting." Very annoying! I have the option on my gmail of
changing messages to rich or plain text formatting when I reply to
them. Your email package may have a similar function that you can use
until messages start coming in plain text.
