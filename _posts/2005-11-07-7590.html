The Dragon Tree group here in So California has been a God send for
our family. My husband and I decided to unschool after 1 1/2 years of
homeschooling and we needed to find a group of people of like mind.

Our "friends" at church, who are very traditional homeschoolers, have
stopped calling and they are not involving us in any activities. My
dd told me that her friends said they can't sleep over at our house
now because I let my dd do whatever she wants.

Yes, I have become a radical unschooler and I miss my friendships, but
our new friends are very supportive of our choice and understand our
mindset. That is what is more important to us than some friends who
are not open to our new outlook on life and learning.

Actually my dd has one of her knew Dragon Tree friends over for a
sleepover tonight.

Kay
