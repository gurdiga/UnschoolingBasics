My kids were the same way! They didn't want to go sit on some creepy
stranger's lap! And I remember telling other moms that and they'd tell stories
about the kids crying and how funny the pictures were. It must be awful for some
kids having their parents force them to do that.

My relatives gave me a hard time, too, for breaking that photo tradition, now
that I think about it. They'd bug the kids, "Don't you want your picture
taken with Santa?" and "He might not bring you something if you don't go talk to
him." And my cousin would *use* it with her kid, that whole "You'd better do
this or that or Santa won't bring you anything."

Ick. I'm so glad we're past that.

My 12yo son and I just watched "Bad Santa" the other night, where Billy Bob
Thornton plays an alcoholic criminal who uses his Santa job to rob department
stores, and he recalled how he never wanted to go sit on Santa's lap. He said
"See?" LOL

-Patty
