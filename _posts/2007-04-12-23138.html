We raise Brittanys and sell children's products. Nice mix huh?
If you went to Peabody Kelly mentioned our dog business in one of her
sessions which I thought was pretty awesome!

There should be a "home" link on the page so you can see what they look like
older than a few minutes.

Several years of 1-2 litters a year and holding the camera while trying to
make sure everyone is breathing and not being crushed by mom who is now
exhausted doesn't net many great images. I love the paw and detail on the sac also.
I could do without the icky one but in order to show the process it kinda
needs to be there. Or shall I say I fear someone taking issue with that one. I
have never disallowed my the moms from eating them.

In one of those images is a male pup who was not moving or breathing. I
think the last left. The shake down technique and some suction worked well along
with bit of panic:) I've never lost a puppy so when he began moving I was
yelling pongo and perty 8 puppies! A day whelp with all the kids home and me
alone was interesting. Cameron our 3 yr old was in the whelping box beside me
gasping in awe of it all.

They are so cute!
Mom and pups are doing wonderful.
