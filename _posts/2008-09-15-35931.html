> I think at some point before Live and Learn Ren mentioned that there
were people in this group that did counseling less expensively than
Naomi Aldort.~~

I charge $25 per session with some follow-up emails free. A session is
about 1/2 hour, with some leeway on time for the first one.:)

I have found that if I respond to everyone that asks, without any fee
whatsoever, it takes too much time from my family. I already work
outside the home and this is one way I can support my family as I work
towards being back at home and earning an income.

The sessions are very support based and designed around your specific
needs at the time. Some people just want a friendly voice to reaffirm
their choices, other people are very "stuck" and need a shift in
perspective.

I've worked with people on parenting issues but also on excavating
their authentic voice and/or creative self. It's very free flowing.

Ren
