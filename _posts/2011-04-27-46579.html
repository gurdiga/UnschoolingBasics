Apologies if this has already been mentioned, but I haven't seen it on here. For UK/European unschoolers - there is a great 1 day unschooling conference happening this June in London .... see the website below for more info and to book tickets, Carole ............

The LiTTLe Conference in London
(Learning Trust - Trusting Learning)

On 11th June 2011 there will be a conference in London for autonomous home educators / unschoolers. It is a day of talks and discussions for parents who are taking a whole life approach to autonomous learning / unschooling.

The speakers are Mike Fortune-Wood, Sandra Dodd, Harriet Pattison & Alan Thomas, Imran Shah, David Waynforth and Schuyler Waynforth.

Full details can be found at the conference website which is

www.lttl.org.uk
