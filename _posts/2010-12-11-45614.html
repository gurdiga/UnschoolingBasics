Hey All,
My family is planning a trip to India. Jan of 2012 - 13 months from now. We'd like to welcome any interested fellow unschoolers to join us in our adventure!
Very briefly: (it's all still in the planning process)3-6 week trip. On our way to India, stop for a week in Europe (depending on the flight route). Then fly into Mumbai and take a car or train to Surat, where my parents live, and stay there a week, get over our jetlag, acclimatize to india, eat yummy food, hang out with locals, for about a week. (this is what we do on our regular trips, for the whole duration!). Then we can head south by train - Goa, then the beaches of Kerala, the southern tip of India called Kanyakumari, and end up in Chennai (formerly known as Madras). Take a flight from there to Delhi, from where we go to Agra, where the Taj Mahal is, then back to Delhi for the flight out of India.
For more details, ongoing discussions, and any questions, please join the travel grouphttp://groups.yahoo.com/group/TRAVULL/
Happy travels!-neha
