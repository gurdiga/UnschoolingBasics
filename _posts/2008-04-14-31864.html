I just have to pass along this show on the Sundance Channel. It is
called Iconoclasts, and it features an American Icon with another type
of American Icon, together in quiet conversation.

The one I saw was Maya Angelou and Dave Chappelle (comedian). Maya
was so amazing, tears streamed down my face the entire hour. I
realized how much our children learn from the adults around them, and
how perhaps bringing more awareness into our home about these amazing
gems is what I need to do! Our elders, and I mean OUR elders are
veritable warehouses of knowledge and stories. And from those stories
the world is alive!

Here you can see the line up.
