Tina,

What I like about it is there are only 5 lines for each date, so I can
write an entry in a few minutes and I don't feel like I have to set
aside a lot of time to do it.
There are also scattered quotes throughout also. Here's one I like:
"I awoke this morning with devout thanksgiving for my friends, the old
and the new" -Ralph Waldpo Emerson.
I don't use it everyday. I use it when I feel like. I just open to that
date and write something I am grateful for. I think part of the reason
I don't use it everyday is that we have a "grateful wall" in our house
and we use that instead.
