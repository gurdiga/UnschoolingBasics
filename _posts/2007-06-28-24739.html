30 Jun 2007, at 8:56am

We're here now! Actually leaving this morning to pick Ben up from the
St Paul-Minneapolis airport before driving all the way to Bemidji to
visit the Traaseths.

This was a wonderful stop! Alex and her family are so welcoming and
entertaining! Her children are adorable---and I haven't been around
little ones for a while! It's fun to watch them play!

The coolest thing, of course (we're at a dairy farm, remember? <g>),
was the birth of a heifer this morning! Brian knocked on my door at
6:30 to say that Allright was calving. I got there just after the head
poked through and got to watch her be born! So exciting! Duncan was too
tired to wake up, but I took several photos of them.

We got to bottle fed a couple of the day-old calves as soon as we got
here---it's just so much fun for me to be at a farm---I LOVE all the
LIFE here! The Moo-serenade all day (with the birds in the
background)---just so peaceful and relaxing. Well, *Brian* doesn't
relax! That has to be the hardest working man I've ever met! <G> But he
loves what he does, and it shows!

We've had a really nice time, and I've thoroughly enjoys metting Alex
and her family (and we got to talk dogs and be dog-snobs together.
Brian is a cow-snob! <BWG>).

You'll have a wonderful time! Promise!

(OH! Don't miss the little Minnesota zoo---critters native to Minnesota
in a lovely, natural setting.)


~Kelly
