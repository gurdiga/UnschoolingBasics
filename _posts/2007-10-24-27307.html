Ria,

It's been years since I switched our two basenjis over to a raw diet so I can't track down the sites that were the most useful to me at the time, but one book I found invaluable (straightforward, no nonsense, easy to follow) was "Switching to Raw" by Sue Johnson.
http://www.switchingtoraw.com/

There are also a number of Yahoo groups out there so you may want to check on those as well. They are a great resource if you have questions, run into problems, etc.

Have fun with your new pup!
