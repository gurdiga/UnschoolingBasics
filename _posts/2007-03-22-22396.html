the truth about unschooling is that it is not a stop and start process.
There is no age limitation, no age requirement, no age restriction. You are
not in or out of it...life is it - you are it! this is not to say that
things won't change or evolve...in fact they very much will. your childs
interests may or may not change, but what will change, as they get older, is
the way in which they persue their interests. It is your job to stay
attentive so you can respond as your child changes and grows and reaches out
for new ways of exploring.

as a family who has always unschooled there was no difference in how we
lived our life when my child turned 'school' age. the differences on how
she approaches learning comes from her drives, desires and abilities. it is
amazing to witness the growth that organically occurs that has nothing to do
with anyone elses time frame or expectations. no one can predict how it
will unfold for each child.
