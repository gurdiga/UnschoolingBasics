26 Jul 2007, at 3:04pm

I surprised the gang the other day with a new game from Cranium called
"Zooreka" and I have to say that it is awesome! We (3, 6, 8 and
parents) had a blast and it is ranked one of our top games and that says
a lot because we love games and have quite a few. You collect food,
animal and shelter cards to trade in for habitats and you need four
habitats to win. You name your zoo when you're done. My kids love Zoo
Tycoon on the computer so this was a love at first play! We even tend
to buy a majority of cooperative games but this one is fun for everyone
even if you don't win. We still let the kids who didn't win create the
rest of their zoos afterward.

A winner in our house and cost $14.96 at Wal Mart.
