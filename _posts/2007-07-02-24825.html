1. Many of you have met or know of my son Julian through my posts. He has
unschooled for most of his life, and is beginning a career as a musician. He's a
singer, songwriter, and guitar player. He wants to be a successful
professional musician, and we're supporting him in making his dreams come true -- and
we'd love your help.

We've made a new list to help Julian become famous. This is the description:
Julian Baptista is a young singer, songwriter, and guitarist who has
unschooled for most of his life. He is beginning his career as a musican, and would
like to do it the way he's lived his life -- free-range, creatively, and
with joy, learning and living in the world.

This group is for people who would like to support Julian in his dream of
becoming a well-known, respected, creative, and self-supporting musician. How
will you help? You might provide ideas, reources, contacts, produce or attend
a house concert, go to a gig, buy a cd, offer a place to crash, tell your
friends -- who knows!

Join us in helping Julian realize his dreams! Subscribe by sending e-mail
to:
_JulianBaptista-subscribe@yahoogroups.com_
(mailto:JulianBaptista-subscribe@yahoogroups.com) If you want to hear a bit of his music go here:
_http://myspace.com/julianbaptista_ (http://myspace.com/julianbaptista)

2. We want to help other unschoolers make their dreams come true, too!
After my Funshop on that topic at last year's Live and Learn conference, I
promised to make a list to do that, and finally, it's here!!! This group is for
brainstorming, resources, and support as all ages of unschoolers work to reach
their dreams. To subscribe, send e-mail to:
_unschoolerwishlist-subscribe@yahoogroups.com_ (mailto:unschoolerwishlist-subscribe@yahoogroups.com)

Thank you for reading this long post!
