It's almost time for the 2nd annual Learn Nothing Day and the Johnson City unschoolers invite you to join us for a celebration in TN. We'll meet at Rock Creek just like last year for a picnic, swimming and general fun on July 24th at noon.

Overnight guests should check out the Comfort Inn as it's conveniently located and inexpensive. Earthfare is just down the road too!

Plan on bringing food, drinks, swimming gear etc... There is hiking available, bbq pits and bathrooms/changing areas. There is a $2 day fee when you get to the park (self service payment as you drive in) so bring a bit of cash. See you there!!
