My boys have both gone months without a bath, LOL. It isn't *sad* at all.
They are happy, healthy boys. My oldest is reaching a point where his hair
gets greasy if he doesn't bathe more often so he is wanting to wash his hair
more regularly. I can say that I don't worry about the *germs or dirt* they
carry. I believe their bodies are used to it, can handle it. They are not
sick any more than any of their friends, so I believe they are not picking up
any more unmanageable bacteria or viruses than other children.

Maybe their bodies are stronger because they come in contact with more
"dirt" who knows. But I choose not to live in fear but instead to seek out the
best information I can from different sources and make informed decisions. I
encourage my children to do the same, and am more than happy to help them
gather information. I have so much to say on the "teeth" issue also but no time
to type it all out. But I think that fear based decision making in any
situation is not a good thing for me or for my children.
