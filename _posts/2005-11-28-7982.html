This jumped out at me because I, too, have a son whose dearest dream was to graduate high school and sign up -- his only plan was to be a Marine. Then at 16, he quit school, led us all into homeschooling and one day announced he wanted to find out how to become a conscientious objector! Totally walked away from any plan to be in the military or go to war, and began to question whether he would even be capable of risking that he might kill another person, even in a time of war.

My point is that his dreams/plans may change. If they don't and he still wants to go into the military, and having a real diploma (vs a GED) is truly necesary, he could always go back to school then. There's nothing to stop him from unschooling now and choosing school in his senior year, or thru an alternative high school, or an online, charter or private school.

just some things to consider.
