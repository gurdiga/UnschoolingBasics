I just wanted to share... especially for all of the newbies who struggle
with implementing unschooling fully and who may or may not realize that
personal growth is the key to doing so.

I started reading "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People: Powerful Lessons
in Personal Change" by Stephen R. Covey. I was in awe of how his book fit
right into unschooling. If you're having a tough time grasping the
philosophies of unschooling and non-coercive parenting and/or you "get it"
but don't know how to make the change to fully implement it, I highly
recommend that you read this book.

(If you've read it, I'd like to hear your opinion about it.)
