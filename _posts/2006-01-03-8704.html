"I also still need to let go of my own schooly fears- I can't wait
until the day when those are gone!"

Well, maybe they'll be gone, maybe not. We all have certain things
surface, due to the lovely job our schooling did on us...but that
doesn't mean our kids have to know this.

When something surfaces in your mind, turn it over, examine it and see
if it has any validity or if it's just some old tape that needs to be
shut off....then smile at your child and move forward. They don't even
have to know you just did a small internal dialogue, or examination of
thoughts. It does get better, but in the mean time, don't let the
fears make you feel badly...just keep them to yourself.;)
