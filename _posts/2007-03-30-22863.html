I think a more relevant question is, what does anything have to NOT do
with RU?

RU isn't about what to do/not to do, so much as encouraging people to
find their own answers, to educate themselves, and to become confident
in their ability to always do so.

Educating one's self about vaccines can be a valuable part of
unschooling... to learn the risks and rewards of each vaccination and
each disease that it protects against, so that a person can decide for
themselves which, if any, vaccinations make sense for them.
