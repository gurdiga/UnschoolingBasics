5 Aug 2008, at 10:16am

L-E-A-P Not Back To School 2008 Picnic to be held Monday September 8th with a rain date
of Tuesday September 9th at Odiorne State Park, Rye , NH.
http://www.nhstateparks.com/odiorne.html Please join us! Put your info in the data base
telling us who you are, how many of you are coming and what you are bringing. There will be
the usual famous LEAP potluck, NBTS '08 t-shirts available for purchase, L-E-A-P crocs
charms available for purchase, TONS of other unschoolers, home schoolers, and out of school
for a long time now type people! Bring chairs, blankets, coolers, drinks, something to share
for the pot luck table. You might also want to bring frisbees, kites, balls, or other group
games. There is a play ground, bathroom facilities, a bike trail, the ocean and of course all
the wonderful LEAP'ers! We may even have face painting like last year!
