If it is the one I am thinking of, it took kids into a deserted, old western style town. There are no adults to supervise. they had to decide who would cook, who lived in which bunk, who were the town council, kill their own food, etc.

And the parents had to sign a major legal paper giving the studio complete authority over their children, including making all medical decisions. In addition, the contract said the studio could not be held liable for any accidents, deaths or STDs. These kids are from about 6 years old to maybe 14.
