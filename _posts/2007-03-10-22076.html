Yes I do know at least one family who is unschooling in Hawaii. And, it
really doesn't look all that difficult - more than WA but not that bad.
File notice when you get there (but it's not annual, just once when you
start); testing OF YOUR CHOICE at grades 3, 5, 8 and 10 (so you can use
something like PASS which is designed to be done at home however you
want pretty much, and an annual progress report which can be a written
evaluation from the parent. So, keeping a journal and then translating
it once a year would be most of what you need to do most years. In the
testing years, no written evaluation because of the testing. I suppose
it may be pretty locally variable so I'd want to touch base with some
folks there (I'd bet there's a yahoo group for homeschoolers if not
unschoolers who could help you with the ropes).

Deb
