There's a new group, PanamaUnschoolers, created for unschoolers either
currently living in Panama or considering a move there. Expats and
native Panamanians welcome!

If you know of any unschooling families who fit this description,
please forward this to them!

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/PanamaUnschoolers/

This is not to take away from any other group, but just to help
unschoolers in Panama (or interested in Panama) find each other for
support, playgroups, field trips, etc.

Thanks!
