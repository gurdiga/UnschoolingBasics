I also have an unschooling friend whose daughter was having problems as a
toddler. Once at the doctor's they were taking blood from her dd, and the
nurse asked if she wanted to test for allergies while doing the blood work.

Once they eliminated these things from her diet she said is was like a whole
new child. Definitely worth looking in to.
