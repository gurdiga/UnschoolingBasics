11 May 2009, at 12:19pm

Dayna Martin's book, "Radical Unschooling - A Revolution Has Begun"
will be published in less than a month!!

Dayna wrote this about her book:

"The book is about Radical Unschooling philosophy and how I believe
it is the evolution of parenting. I sprinkle personal stories and
examples from our own life to share how kids do learn as a side
effect of living a joyful life pursiing their passions."

John Taylor Gatto wrote this about the book,

"Full of brilliant common sense! Mrs. Martin takes us behind the
curtain to see in rich detail what alternative education really looks
like when it bestows on its subjects the freedom to choose."

For details and ordering info, see her blog:

http://thesparklingmartins.blogspot.com/2009/05/radical-unschooling-
revolution-has.html

I'm so excited!
