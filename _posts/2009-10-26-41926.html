Hello Ladies of BirthNet NY,
I hope this finds you all well. As my husband and I began to prepare for our
Dec homebirth, we realized how little was out there by way of children's
literature for our nearly 2yr old who will be there for the big day, so I
have written/illustrated and self-published a children's book for
homebirthing families and wanted to share the website with you. I am getting
great feedback from midwives/doulas and families who are finding it to be a
fun, informative, useful and very affordable tool for children whose
families are planning a homebirth...which is fantastic!
www.homebirthchildrensbook.com

As you well know, the homebirthing community is strong and tight, although
rather small. I encourage you to share the link with anyone and everyone who
you think can benefit from it, and I thank you in advance for doing so.

All the very best,
Kelly Mochel
www.homebirthchildrensbook.com

PS. In addition to the positive review on my website, ICAN is set to publish
a positive review in the next issue of the Clarion...which we are thrilled
about!
