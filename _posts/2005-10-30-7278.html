I googled bumper stickers, went to the different sites and
chose "education" for the theme of the stickers. You will get several
stickers regarding different viewpoints on education but I could
usually find some good unschooling ones from each site. I also have a
lot that aren't necessarily unschooling but they express the
sentiment, such as: Those of us dancing are thought strange by those
who can't hear the music, things like that. I got most of mine from
the following online sites: cafepress.com, northernsun.com (they will
send you a catalog when you order with some cool stuff in it), and
pegasuspublishing.com. I also got some of mine at the conference, Hot
Topic, and Bosco (a card-like shop that sells D&D and such). Maybe I
will post a picture of the back of my van, give me a little bit though
because I am not real organized, as we are getting ready to move into
our motorhome for a year.
