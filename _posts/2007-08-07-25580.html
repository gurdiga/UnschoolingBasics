Conference 2007:
Empowering, Encouraging, and Exciting
Home Educators



Saturday, October 6, 2007
USNH, 700 Hartford Turnpike, Hamden, CT


Keynote Speaker: Peter Kowalke

Peter Kowalke is the producer and editor of Grown Without Schooling.
Released in 2002, Grown Without Schooling was one of the first documentaries
produced about homeschooling. Peter currently writes about grown
homeschoolers for Life Learning Magazine. He has also been a columnist for
Home Education Magazine and Home Educator's Family Times, and speaks
regularly at homeschooling conferences. In 1997 was the subject of a feature
article in the New York Times Sunday Magazine.


Keynote Presentations:

*
Trusting Your Child's Inner Compass

*
Screening of the Grown Without Schooling documentary

*
Grown Homeschooler Panel Discussion


Additional conference sessions will all be
"Empowering, Encouraging and Exciting"

CHN's mission statement is "to inform, empower, and foster community among
home educators in the state of Connecticut", and this conference will focus
on that goal with sessions designed to provide information, answer
questions, and allow participants to dialog with presenters and get their
questions answered. Sessions you will have the opportunity to attend
include:
