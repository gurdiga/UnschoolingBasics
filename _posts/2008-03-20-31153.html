Have you ever been to a Live & Learn Conference? Have you benefitted from
listening to the downloads of the speakers? Are you one of the hundreds of
people whose lives have been enhanced by this magnificent conference? Do you
want to contribute in a meaningful way to very last Live & Learn Conference?
This is your chance. No matter whether you are able to attend or not, you
can still share in a meaningful way that will benefit many people.

"How?" you ask?

You can donate to the Raffle, Passion Basket Silent Auction and an Instant
Gratification table. All of these events raise money for the Scholarship
program. This is our chance to go out with a record contribution! Let's go
nuts!

The Raffle includes any and all items. Contribute some art, music, toys,
books, jewelry, games or clothes. Items do not have to be brand new, but
they need to be in great condition. Hand-made items are also a hit.

Would you like to make a special package of themed items for our silent
auction? Then contribute a Passion Baskets. Here are a few examples:

Beekeeper Basket-a bee bonnet filled with gloves, a smoker, a hive tool, a
book or two, some Burt's Bees products, two beeswax candles, and a jar of
homemade honey.

Movie Basket- a dvd, microwave popcorn, six pack of root beer, some Heath
bites, and a gift certificate for a national pizza chain in a large popcorn
bowl.

The Instant Gratifications table needs small toys, temporary tattoos,
bubbles, stickers and any other kid-friendly items. Got a bunch of old fast
food meal toys you need to unload? Did your child clean out their toy box
and feel ready to let go of some stuff? Send it in and give a child some
instant gratification!

Want more information? Have a question? Please contact me!
