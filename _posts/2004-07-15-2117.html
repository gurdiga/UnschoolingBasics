Does everyone know about All Round Magazine? It's a beautiful hand-drawn
magazine which only comes out twice a year, but is worth the wait. We've
subscribed for a few years and are always amazed by the new issue. Another
big plus is that every submission my dd has made has been published. If
you have a child who is forever disappointed to not have a letter or
drawing published in a magazine like Spider or Ranger Rick you'll see what
a plus this is. I do not have any other connection to the magazine other
than being a subscriber, but since we just got our latest issue today I
thought I'd mention it. My dd also has a drawing in this issue and she is
so excited.

www.allroundmagazine.com

They even have links on the website for unschooling- very cool!!
Kristin
