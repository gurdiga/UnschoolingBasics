My daughter is involved with a great group called The American Heritage
Girls.

My daughter is 10 and this is her first year doing it.

It's a Christian -like scout troop.



We work on service projects, badges, and have a lot of fun. I like it sooo
much better than Girl Scouts but if you don't want a Christian group, then
its not for you.

If you would like more info, let me know.



Each badge that you earn, you learn sooo much about.

For example, so far, my daughter has earned her cake decorating badge and
her group is working on Personal Hygiene badge. She is also doing the
scrapbook one on her own.
