I have made reservations at Sampson State Park for the above dates. My
intention is to create a space for unschoolers/radical unschoolers (and
those of similar mind, open to, and/or interested in unschooling) to be
together informally for a couple days.

You are invited to come camp for the weekend. We live just outside the
park and the sites are about a half mile walk/bike ride from our home
via a park road that empties out into our neighborhood.

I have reserved site #28 loop 1 for Friday-Sunday, Sept 17-19. There
are currently numerous vacant sites immediately adjacent to ours that
you can reserve at the link below.

This is a self-directed weekend where you'll need to bring everything
you need or want with you, including activities. Make your reservation
at the site below and send me your email so you can be part of
pre-weekend discussions. My role is to put out a date and location and
to host a pre-weekend conversation. As the date gets closer I will keep
in touch with those who reply they are joining us to discuss what we
want to do while together. This may (or may not) include shared meals or
activities, or whatever else will be of interest to those coming.

Please reply to me at the email below when you make your reservations so
I can add you to the conversation.

You are welcome to come up for the day as well, just let me know when
you'd like to join us so we can include you.

My intention is for further planning conversations to not clutter the
various lists I'm announcing this on. I'll post additional announcements
between now and the weekend on the lists, but please move pre-weekend
conversations off-list among those attending. If you aren't sure you can
attend but want to be part of that discussion, sent me an email and
you'll be included.

I hope to see many of you there for the weekend.
