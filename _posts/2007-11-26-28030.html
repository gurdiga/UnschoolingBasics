Greetings from the 2008 Unschooling Conference in Madison, Wisconsin.

Our 2007 conference, the first unschooling conference ever in Wisconsin, was a huge success! It was a very energizing and renewing experience. Participants took full advantage of the small size of the conference and the intimate atmosphere to connect and have in-depth conversations with other conference attendees and presenters. Feedback was very enthusiastic, and many people asked for another conference in 2008, so here we go.
