This is related to unschooling, but not specifically about
unschooling. I just want to offer up an invitation to my "imagination
tribe" creativity list for anyone interested.

It's not an unschooling list, anyone is welcome to join. The purpose
of that list is to encourage your creative voice, to give a safe place
for people to explore their own creative genius (or give resources to
help you re-claim that creative self if it's been damaged).

We're in the midst of planning an ATC trade in the mail. You (and
anyone you think would benefit from such a list) are welcome to join us.
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/imaginationtribe/

The Imagination Tribe is the group responsible for creating that
lovely Circle Journal some of you may have seen at the conference.:)
I'm going to try and keep some cool ideas available, so members have a
chance to try new things and come up with new ways to express themselves.
It's a lot of fun!
