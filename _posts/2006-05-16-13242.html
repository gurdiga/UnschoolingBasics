Hi Sabrina,



Welcome. Where in Arizona are you? I'm in Tucson & also have a 7 yr old,
as well as a 10 yr old.



Arizona is a great place to unschool! You can go to tucsonhomeschooling.org
and check out "Arizona regulations" on the left hand side.

The law says you need to file an Affidavit of Intent to homeschool and send
it to your county's school superintendent. You might want to take it in
person because you also need to show the child's birth certificate.

And that's it!



You might also want to check out the Home Education Network of Arizona at
www.hena.us <http://www.hena.us/>
