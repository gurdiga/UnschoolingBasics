The picture is here:

http://dragonwritingprompts.blogsome.com/2006/05/02/whats-gnu/

Write a conversation between then as they're off to wherever they're
going.

Things to think about if you're stuck:

Who are these guys?
Where are they headed?
What is their goal?
What is their purpose?
Are they unique in their world?
Were they created? By who?
Did they come from somewhere else? Where and what is it like there?
Why did they come?

Joyce
