<http://www.greatbighappylife.com/> Great Big Happy Life- An Unschoolers'
Conference Retreat



The conference site is a gorgeous camp in Lebanon, New Jersey- it has nature
trails, a lake, an awesome creek for creek hikes, great stars, beautiful
grounds and wifi too! As with so many other unschooling conferences that
have taken root in the past few years, this one was inspired by the Live and
Learn Conference that just had its final magnificent run in September '08.
Great Big Happy Life will be a blend of inspirational presentations, awesome
participant led funshops, scavenger hunts, informal discussions, intense
relaxation and just generally hanging out with other unschooling families.
Join us!
