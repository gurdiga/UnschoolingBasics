I'm "getting" more unschoolish as the days go by...
My 2 little girls went with me to help pick out fresh ingredients to
accompany supper - that's nothing really new, they get to pick the ones
they think are the prettiest and tastiest (green peppers, cucumbers,
fresh salad, etc)

One thing I did differently that I didn't realize I'd not been doing
much ... They went to the bulk section and asked for a candy necklace
each. I bought them (there was no money issues, they just wanted 35c
worth of candy in total), and I didn't make them "wait until after
dinner".... They still ate their dinner joyfully, and enjoyed the whole day.
