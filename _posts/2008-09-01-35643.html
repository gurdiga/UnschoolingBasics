Hi everyone!
so much of my unschooling journey in the last two years has been inspired by this list. Before I went out and found people in the area, this group was my way to relax and learn, and truly felt like having a good and inspiring time with friends.
Even now, that we have made many friends in the area, I still go to this group for inspiration and to check in from time to time. Now I have felt the wish to document some of our journey in the unschooling lifestyle and since Unschooling Basics has been so paramount in this process I would love to have a chance to meet - and possibly interview and film - people on this list that will be at the Live and Learn conference. I have looked at the schedule, and for me either thursday morning after 10:30 before lunch or friday morning after 10:00 would work for me.
From what I can gather it seems that Heaton Hall, 1st floor is mostly available. Please let me know if anyone would love to meet and participate -
From what I understand, Kelly, you started this conference also in the wish to meet people from this list?
Some of you know that I will be filming some footage in the hopes of showing it on German TV in combination with a lot of filming on the German homeschooling movement. For this particular shoot I mostly like to interview young people, and hope to find them around.
In addition to that I would love to make an inspiring and fun short movie on Unschooling Basics, the online group, it's stor, as well as the concepts, so any and all participation in this is highly welcome!!!
A lot of you guys are probably on the road already, I will be hopefully posting a meeting somewhere.
All the best,
Mara

children's lives other than Trevor trying kindergarten.

So.....a lot of the orignal people we found at the message board are
still with us at this list. There have been a lot of newer folks from
this list come out to the conference also! And sometimes it's worked
the other way around...someone hears about the list at a conference
and joins.

Sandra Dodd was a key figure at the unschooling dotcom message board,
and several of us learned a lot from her lists she started. She and
Joyce Fetteroll and Pam Sorooshian and Deb Lewis and Rue Kream (am I
forgetting anyone?) were some of the key people who influenced both
Kelly and I in our early journey.
