UNSCHOOLERS WINTER WATERPARK GATHERING UPDATE:

Were excited to announce that well now be having unschooling talks & discussions, funshops, social gatherings, etc.., at our Unschooler's Winter Waterpark Gathering!.

The Kalahari Waterpark Resort, is now allowing us to use some of their conference rooms (for free!) throughout our gathering. We have 2 moms coordinating events, funshops, unschooling discussion, social activities, ect So, our families will have an additional way to now connect with other unschoolers. So, theyll be plenty to do, throughout the week for both the kids and parents!

Unschoolers from around the country and Canada will be gathering for our first Winter Waterpark Gathering. Please join our yahoo group today, for further details and information on how to register, while there are still rooms available. http://groups.yahoo.com/group/UWWG/
