The list we left was called _unschooling-101@yahoogroups.com_
(mailto:unschooling-101@yahoogroups.com) . I believe its new name is
_HEMNewHomeschoolers@yahoogroups.com_ (mailto:HEMNewHomeschoolers@yahoogroups.com) . I could be
wrong about the new name.
You're welcome to sign up over there and read the archives.
Ren and I felt we would do better to set up an e-list for those who wanted
answers on understanding unschooling. That list was becoming another war zone
and rather anti-unschooling.
Ren and I (and our moderators, Deb and Rue) are pretty much dyed-in-the-wool
unschoolers---we've been called the MOST radical of unschoolers! <g> We
truly believe that unschooling is the best way for a child (and an adult) to grow
up. We are all non-political and could care less about curricula and
inclusivity.
If you want to know more about unschooling, we're your team! We'll work hard
to explain unschooling and gentle, mindful parenting in a fun (and often
humorous) way! <G>
But we don't have time for fighting and whining!
The archives are there (I guess), but to rehash it all on this sparkly new
list would not be a good use of time or screen!
Thanks for understanding!
