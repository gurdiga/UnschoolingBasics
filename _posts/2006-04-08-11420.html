What does it *really* say? I mean literally word by word? It might seem
like it means specific books and resources but it if it is worded, "What
books and resources do you intend to use?" That is very broad. You can
say, "We intend to use a wide spectrum of books too numerous to list from a
multitude of sources including (but not limited to) the public library,
bookstores, online e-books, sharing libraries and our church." (Many
churches have a some sort of lending library and if you attend La Leche
League or other mom groups they usually have some sort of sharing library).
For resources, go look at your bookshelves and see what books are there that
are about science or history and write those down as resources. If you are
in contact with a local homeschooling or unschooling group list that as a
resource. List any books you have read on homeschooling. And state that
you intend to use interest led unit studies. (Isn't that sort of what we
are doing kind of maybe in a way?) :)
