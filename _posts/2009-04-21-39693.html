Hi all,
May be a strange post, but I wanted to let you all know that we have the
chicken pox, or at least my son Nathaniel does. He was not vaccinated and
has a mild case. I know some people may want to expose their children to it
so they can get it over with before they get much older. If you'd like to
expose your child, let me know and we can make arrangements to let them hang
out. He's already starting to crust over with some and isn't developing many
more--the doctor says its a pretty mild case. But it should still be
sufficient for him to develop the antibodies. Anyway, let me know. And feel
free to forward to other local lists.
Best,
Jill
