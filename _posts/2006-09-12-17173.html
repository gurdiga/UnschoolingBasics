12 Sep 2006, at 10:36pm

I just returned from the 5th annual Live and Learn unschooling
conference last night. Suffice it to say, it was a magical and
empowering week...once again. The hotel in Albuquerque was magnificent!

It was so great to see familiar faces and meet some new ones. So much
incredible energy was shared all week and new connections made. I
can't put into words how it feels to be surrounded by your "tribe".
I'm so grateful for this event that has created a diverse and rich
community.

If anyone here missed this year and wants to attend, it's not too
early to start planning for next year!! Asheville NC is next year's
location and it's going to be an awesome event.

I'm going to spend some time processing last week and all the great
things that happened....I'll post to my blog as soon as I get it all
distilled (and some photos developed). It's good to be back online.:)
