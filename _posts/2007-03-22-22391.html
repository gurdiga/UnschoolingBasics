PLAY - be a "student" of your kids. It doesn't need to look anything at
all like "preschool" or whatever. Spend an afternoon messing about in
the mud out in the backyard then have a blast in a nice warm bubble
bath. Kids are good at playing, grown ups tend to loose it. So, start
learning play all over again from your kids. Have fun. Relax. Live,
love, laugh together, the learning will happen, formal didactic type
stuff need never happen (unless one of the kids happens to be fascinated
by textbooks)
