...unschooled one child but not the other? I find myself in that
situation and most days I can make it work but sometimes I want to
pull my hair out. LOL

A little background:
My husband & I adopted our three 2 years ago. The oldest two have
what is called Reactive Attachment Disorder, with my oldest being
the one who is more severe. We are actually considering putting him
in a theraputic group home. My middle one is healing and the
youngest is emotionally healthy. I have unschooled the middle two
since taking them out of school and things have gone from good to
fantastic! :-) Not only do they love being home, they love the
freedom they have and they are thriving and loving every day. :-)

I've posted a bit about my situation on some of the other lists and
also on Pam's NoMoreSpanking List. I'm wondering if anybody has any
advice even though they may not be in my situation. I would love
nothing more than to unschool all of them but it's not possible at
this time and I am always looking for ways to make this work.
