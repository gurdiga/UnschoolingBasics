I just think that is such a much more empowered approach to going to college

Julian, who is 18, is now a Goddard College student. And still unschooling.
He's able to do this because Goddard is extremely non-traditional, and
EVERYTHING comes from Julian. He has total flexibility and options to switch gears
when he wants to, and he is loving it.

It's interesting -- I went to Hampshire College, a non-traditional, hippy
sorta place where everyone designs their own majors. And I adored it, and it
ruined me for traditional education, which is one of the things that led me to
unschooling. That said, for unschoolers who don't care about a regular
residential program, I think Goddard is the place to be. Amazing program (and
considerably more affordable than most other places.)
