"The key is that the more you focus on what you want and FEEL IT as if
you have, the quicker what you desire comes."

Our thoughts are more powerful than we realize. At some point during
my own spiritual evolution, I noticed that the natural dialogue in my
head was mostly negative; "I'm not talented enough to do that", "I'm
too disorganized to get ________accomplished", "people will laugh if I
try_________" etc...
DUMB!!
I was my own worst critic! If we can't be on our own side, then who
can be?? I finally learned to catch that inner critic at work and
replace the knee jerk messages with soothing, positive inner talk.

It's been hugely transformational. Part of it is to phrase everything
as if I already have exactly what it is I desire; "I am successful, I
am brilliant and confident. I have talent, I am a creative genius" etc...
