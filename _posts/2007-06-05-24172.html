We have Dish and we love it!

We have a High Def package and Dish has the most High Def channels right
now. Our kids (almost 6 and 2.5) love Animania on HD. We didn't get
any premium movie channels because we usually end up renting the movies
we want to see before they come out on the movie channels. Plus, there
are free weekends for the movie channels every now and then, and always
a few channels to preview free every month.

I just checked our package and we don't get The Science Channel, so I
can't tell you if it's good or not. You might want to go to their
website (the Science Channel) and see what kinds of shows they offer.
Discovery HD is pretty awesome though, it's what the National Geographic
channel should be.

We have a HD DVR and a regular DVR.

Kristie

chose Dish because it seemed less sport intensive and at the time seemed

Marvels, and occasionally a good International Profiles just to name a
