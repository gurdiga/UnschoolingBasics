Please join us for Great Big Happy Life- An Unschoolers' Conference Retreat
running from Tuesday April 28, 2009- Friday May 1, 2009. The conference is
for everyone and anyone who wants to come -not just New Jerseyans.



The conference site is a gorgeous camp in Lebanon, New Jersey- it has nature
trails, a lake, an awesome creek for creek hikes, great stars, beautiful
grounds and wifi too! We plan to open up early bird registration in the next
few weeks. As with so many other unschooling conferences that have taken
root in the past few years, this one was inspired by the Live and Learn
Conference that just had its final magnificent run in September. Great Big
Happy Life will be a blend of inspirational presentations, awesome
participant led funshops, scavenger hunts, informal discussions, intense
relaxation and just generally hanging out with other whole life/radical
unschooling families. You can visit the website at
www.greatbighappylife.com- we will be updating the site soon so check back
over the next week or two to read about accommodations, fees, programming,
and all that good stuff. We have many cool ideas, and we welcome your input
as we go forward.

or email NJUC-subscribe@yahoogroups.com .



Send ideas, questions, suggestions-we looking forward to seeing you there!
