hi.
i am new here, as you can tell, and i am interested in unschooling.
i was first interested in it after reading "the teenage liberation
handbook" this past fall. i, in my excitment, threw together a
proposal and showed it to my parents.

of course, they were completely against the idea. with my
suggestion, they read "the teenage liberation handbook", let the
idea mull over for a couple weeks and again said no. but instead of
sending me back to public school that year, i ended in a liberal
private school for my first year of highschool.

as the year is winding down, i have looked into the topic of
unschooling again because my school is very dissatisfying
academically (it's been two trimesters and i think i've learned
about three things). i don't want to go back to my old public school
where i learned more but the only things on students minds are sex,
drugs, and cheerleading/football. i also don't want to go to a
preppy private school. so, unschooling seems like the best choice
out of my options so far.

i've been putting together another, more in-depth proposal for my
parents, but do you have any other suggestions so my parents will
agree to unschooling?

thanks,
japaneseprint.
