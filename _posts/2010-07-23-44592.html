Peter Gray, PhD, who has studied and written about natural learning, and who will be speaking at the Northeast Unschooling Conference in August, is asking for your experiences with ADD/ADHD.

http://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/freedom-learn/201007/i-want-your-stories-adhd-in-homeschoolers-unschoolers-and-free-schoolers

I do not have direct experience, but I have heard again and again of unschoolers' ADD being 'cured' as soon as they were out of school and allowed to focus on what they wished to. As a friend of mine put it, "ADD, the only disease completely curable by removing the cause: something boring."
