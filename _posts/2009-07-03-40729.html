Apologies for the off topic post, I hope it is OK to do this very occasionally when something is really important to me?

For those who are not aware, here in Australia there is a new bill
which, if Parliament passes it in its current form, will make it
illegal to have a home birth with an independent midwife in Australia
as of July next year. Please, if you agree that women should be able to
make an informed choice about where they give birth, take a minute to
sign the petition here:
http://www.homebirthaustralia.org/sites/sign-petition-save-private-midwifery
