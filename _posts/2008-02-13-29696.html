Those of you who know me IRL know that I'm a dog nut and that I joke
that the movie, Best in Show is a weird little concentrated microcosm
of my "other" life.

SO---I had a VERY exciting evening last night when the Weimaraner took
a Group I and the beagle went on to win BiS.

I'm a Weimaraner breeder/enthusiast for years now and have served in
several capacities for the Weimaraner Club of America. I'm also an AKC
judge, licensed to judge Junior Showmanship (and a former junior
handler myself) and have completed all the AKC requirements to judge
Weimaraners. My beautiful old male Weim, Luger, took several group
placements in Europe when we lived there.

I'm thrilled to say that the pair that won the Sporting Group last
night, Alessandra Folz and Marge, are very good friends of mine.
Alessandra was one of the junior handlers I worked with when we lived
in the St Louis area back when she was only 10-13 years old. Her
sister, Sossanna, is also a wonderful handler and has shown several of
the family's viszlak to many, many Bests in Show. They are a wonderful
family---and they are over the moon with Marge's win last night. I was
so excited for them that I couldn't get to sleep! <g> I finally talked
with them this morning. I'm still giddy! <BWG>

Marge is a lovely representative of the breed and is always handled to
perfection by Alessandra. (As a side note, Marge also alerts Alessandra
to potential seizures and has saved her life numerous times!)

I really wish they could have taken Best in Show, but as many of you
know, I *DO* adore that beagle too. <G> I have a beagle myself (the
adorable SodaPop) and talk a lot about how damned cute he is. I'm busy
looking for a second beagle now--- I could have a *six-pack* of
beagles! <g>

The beagle, Uno, is a hometown boy, from Lugoff, SC---just a short
drive from my house. I've been drooling over him for about a year now.
He was certainly the crowd favorite! So, as much as I wanted to see
Alessandra and Marge take the whole burrito, Uno's win is exciting for
me too.

Sooo---I'm coming down from that incredible high I was on last night,
and I just wanted to share a small peek into my "other" life. <g>

~Kelly
