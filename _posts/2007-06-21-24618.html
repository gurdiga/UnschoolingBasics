I'm just a lurker, but there was such a HUGE SCREAMING connection I
have to this game, I just had to share it!! I find unschooling has so
many neat connections, but this is one I have a personal connection
to!! My husband was one of the programmers/developers/web designers
for this game :) If you look in the credits of the game (in the about
page) and you see Andrew Saunders, well that's my dear husband. He was
given a wonderful opportunity to be a part of this, while being able
to work at home, so not only was he involved in something we have lots
of fun reading the press about, but he was able to be home with us
since last August even while working.

I always see so many connections in our unschooling life, but this one
brought a great smile to my face that I have a personal one to this
particular thing and I thought I'd share.
