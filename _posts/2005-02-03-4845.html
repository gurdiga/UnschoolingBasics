"When and wheredoes the school work come in with homeschooling???"

With homeschooling...whenever you feel like forcing it.
With unschooling, it doesn't.
We are here to tell you that making a child do schoolwork is
detrimental to real learning. Please check our links section at this
website for resources and recommendations and learn more about
unschooling if you think it's something you'd like to discuss.

This list is to help people get closer to unschooling, to learn more
about unschooling and to discuss ways to help unschooling flow
better in your home. Not how to school kids.

Another thought Latoya,
Your pushing them to bed earlier might actually be causing some
really great learning moments. If the little one is in bed early,
you could spend more time with the older kids. Sometimes our very
best learning moments happen late at night around here! If I made
them go to bed, we'd be missing some amazing moments, not to mention
some lovely nighttime walks when the world is peaceful and dark.:)
