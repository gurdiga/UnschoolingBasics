As the sunlight returns to our days and with it the reminder of the
promise of possibilities. I ask that you join me in holding the World
in a vision of complete Peace and see waves of Understanding and
compassion washing over all.

May you feel the Light of Love always and in all ways.
May you remember every breath is a prayer
May you sense the infinite Possibilities and the wonder of Life,
And always remember, that one person makes all the difference
... and that person
is YOU!

Namaste'
