ice drum.

But we found this isn't easy out here in the boonies... and it isn't cheap,
either.

First you have to dig the water line trench from the pump to the house and
lay your line...

Then you dig out a huge hole for a well house...

Then you build the "in the ground" cement and cinder block well house...

and then Finally, you install the deep well pump and hook it all up!

All this takes time & patience, big guys with backhoes & cement mixers...
and money.
