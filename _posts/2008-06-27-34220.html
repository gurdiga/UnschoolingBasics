Hi everyone,
Our family will be launching our online magazine just for home schooling kids next month! We are looking for area mom leaders in all major cities. You main responsibility will be to report on home schooling events etc. in your area. No experience is needed. The only requirement is a phone (which you will not use much) computer and a great personality.
If interested in representing your city, please contact me at homeschoolingkidsmagazine@... for more information on earning potential and responsibilities.
I look forward to hearing from you!

ALSO...

Looking for Home Schooling Kid Reporters and Article Ideas
We are a Home Schooling family getting ready to launch an online magazine just for Home Schooling kids nationwide! We are looking for a few more Kid Reporters to cover stories monthly or bi-monthly! If interested please submit a sample article from your child (500 words or less) along with your name, email and age to us at homeschoolingkidsmagazine@...
We are also looking for anything and everything Home Schooling related. If your child/family has done something you want to share, please send it to us! We are looking for:
.Participation in community Events/Volunteering
.Awards won
.Recipes (original)
.Crafts (original)
.Short stories
.Cartoons
.Bright Ideas
Please send them all to us at: homeschoolingkidsmagazine@... and attach pictures if you have them.
Due to the volume of email that comes in, please allow 48 hours for a response. Thank you and we look forward to hearing from you!
