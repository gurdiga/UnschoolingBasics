Carolina Homeschooler

14 Nov 2010, at 4:17pm

Hi everyone,

Registration is open for our New York City trips this May and June.
We'll visit Ellis Island and the Statue of Liberty, walk the Brooklyn
Bridge, explore Times Square and watch a Broadway play, get a
great view of the city at Rockefeller Center's Top of the Rock, play
in Central Park, and have time to explore a museum or other site
- your choice. Our groups will be smaller this year, so early
registrants will have a better chance of having confirmed spots for
their preferred weekend. Come join us!

Read more about all of our trips at:
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travel.htm

If you'd like to receive updates about this trip or any of our other trips,
please subscribe to our Traveling Homeschoolers newsletter at:
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/lists.htm

Carolina Homeschooler was created in 2004 as a statewide support
group for South Carolina homeschoolers. Our trips, however, are
open to homeschooling families across the United States and beyond.
We are not a travel agency - we just enjoy traveling and meeting new
families along the way.

Happy Traveling,
Dianna
