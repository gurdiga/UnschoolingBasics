I have only one son, and he never was circumcised.

I'm not Jewish, but part of the lack of circumcision has to do with the
"ignorance is bliss" thing. I didn't know anything about it and that it
was supposed to be an issue when having a baby boy. I never had to do
anything special with my girls so the idea that I might have to do
something special with my boy just never entered my mind.

So, later on, when I discovered the internet and saw all these "Circ vs
Non Circ" threads, it was a whole learning curve for me... I figured,
except in extreme medical situations, most people come with the parts
nature intended, and left it at that.
