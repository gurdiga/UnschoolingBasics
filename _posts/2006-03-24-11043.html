For the family moving to Georgia (sorry-I'm short on time or I'd go back to gt your name!)

HEIR is a grassroots org here in GA that helps homeschoolers and advocates for
homeschooling rights and freedom. Inclusive, non-profit, their website has the GA law,
forms to download, Q&A forum, etc. If you can't find it there ....
Also depending on where you're relocating there are two really good non-sectarian, inclusive
homeschooling groups that offer classes, support, "playgroups"--AAEN, which is north of
Atlanta and LEAD for the Atlanta area. www.leadhomeschool.org , I believe.

Welcome to my neck of the woods,

Robin
