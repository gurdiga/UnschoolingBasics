One of our favorite books is Zen Shorts. It is a fiction book about a
wonderful panda that moves next door to three children and teaches them in
very unschoolish ways through Zen stories. He acknowledges their feelings
and never belittles their mistakes, but uses them as learning moments.
While fiction, it is SO affirming and positive and has led to some wonderful
discussions about real life situations we have faced.
