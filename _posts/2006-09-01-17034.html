Tracy, as Ren said you might want to consider heading over to P'cola
on Wednesdays for our LIFE group. There are a few of us unschoolers
still holding out amongst the group. Although it isn't all
unschoolers everyone is respectful. Lots of gentle parenting and child
lead interest activities. We meet Wednesday from 11-2 at the Bayview
Community Center (LMK if you need directions). If you go to the
website for the community center it actually says we meet on Tuesdays.
We've notified them of the error and they are trying to fix it. They
know we have met there for going on 5 years on Wednesdays so they
aren't sure why it says Tuesday. You can give me a call if you want
to talk or just get together. (474-0817) We are losing the community
center for 2 weeks starting the Wednesday after this one as they are
stripping the floors in the gym and it is quite toxic (which makes me
curious as to what they do with all the school children who are in
after school care?) We hopefully are either going to go to the
Environmental Center or the Silver Screen movie theater (for a tour
and movie and lunch). One week we are heading out to the beach as one
of our "seasonal" members has rents a condo out there each fall and
invites us over.

Hope to see you there. Oh if you want to check the group out we have
a yahoogroup called PensacolaLIFEHomeschool

Hey Tracy,

Sorry I am just now replying to this, but we just got back from vacation! I live in Alabama, but we live closer to B'ham. My dd is 12yro and although we've homeschooled for about 2 1/2 yrs now, we just started unschooling in April. There is also an Alabama Unschooler yahoo group, but not a lot goes on there (nothing like this group!). We just got back from the Rethinking Education Conference in Texas and we were the ONLY family from AL! We wanted to go to the Live and Learn conference too but our schedule didn't permit this year, but next year we want to do both conferences AND send our dd to Not Back To School Camp. I hope we have the money for all of it. I hope you can connect with other unschoolers in your area, we don't know any other unschoolers and I thought the conference was great b/c we could be with other freedom loving families! It's hard to establish a support system when you're the only ones you know that do what you do!

Best Wishes!
Leslie
