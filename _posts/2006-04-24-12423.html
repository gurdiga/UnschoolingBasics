If you can find it, look for "More Bugs in Boxes" or maybe it is "Bugs in
Boxes." It's sort of hard to find and we ended up ordering it from some
obscure place online. It is a FUN and funny program that most toddlers can
do easily. there is matching and song making and other games that are just
really fun to do. This was Mary Elayne and Keon's favorite game to play for
the longest time. Emily even enjoyed it when we first got it. When it died
and we had hunt for the second one, Dan made a copy of it and we kept the
original safe up on a shelf. It's a fun and not nearly as annoying game as
some other games meant for toddlers. And the bugs are all cute and funny,
not realistic. The weird thing is that the kids haven't played it in a long
time but I can still remember some of the songs. :)
