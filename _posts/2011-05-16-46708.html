Space Camp Homeschool Trip

September 2-4, 2011



Join Carolina Homeschooler in September for an exploration of space,
astronomy, and technology in Space Camp's Pathfinder program in Huntsville,
Alabama. During this 3-day program for students in 4th grade and above (and
their parents), we'll experience training simulators and complete a Space
Shuttle mission, tour the US Space & Rocket Center, learn about space
history, and see first-hand what it takes to become an astronaut. The
registration deadline is May 31.



To find out more, please visit
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelspa.htm



To receive announcements about all of our trips, subscribe to our mailing
list at http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/lists.htm
