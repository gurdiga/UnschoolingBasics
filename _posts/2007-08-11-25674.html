11 Aug 2007, at 2:06pm

Last night at 11pm we were all asleep as far as I know... At least I
was. It's the MORNING PEOPLE [:D]

Seriously though. Since the heat in the middle of the day has been just
unbearable this past week we have started getting up around 5 to work in
the early cool of the morning. Yesterday about the time it got
unbearable we all got in the river and went for a wade. Ended up walking
about two miles upstream looking for wood to carve and other fun things
like frogs and snakes. The boys found a ring-necked snake, some turkey
feathers, and a baby squirrel that had fallen from a nest. They are
taking turns nursing that squirrel so I guess some one was up tending
the poor thing. I'm hoping it will live but doubtful. His little eyes
are not open yet so they are bottle feeding him.

12 Aug 2007, at 1:27pm

Friday night when you posted this, I was too tired to write! We were
getting up early to head over to Hickory NC for a homeschooling
conference Saturday morning. So I'll just post some random stuff
that's been going on over here lately.

The conference was a lot of fun and we met some new folks from the
lists. The NC crew did a great job organizing that event, I imagine
the attendance will be even higher next year. I got to hang out with
Kelly too...that's always great!

My kids had a ball playing and reconnecting with some of their
friends. We hung out at the Asheville mall on the way home. We left
Trevor there, with Moira's family (his gf) and we won't see him for a
week. It's weird having him gone more lately.

The rest of us picked up some Cinnabons which are making the house
smell lovely this morning. Ciara and Jalen have been building box
"houses" for the baby kitties and re-arranging their area over and
over. Jalen gets this veeerrrry high-pitched squeaky voice when he
talks to the kittens. It cracks us up.

We're getting ready to celebrate Jared's birthday tomorrow, with a
friend that has a birthday close to him. We'll have an airsoft battle
in the backyard. I'm trying to have a quiet, calm morning after a
hectic week so I can re-charge before we run around for party supplies.

There is an Imagination Tribe trade to work on, a floor to mop and
snacks to buy. Ciara and I will do our soapy-water--all-over-the-floor
mopping. Lotsa fun.:) We get lots of soapy water and splash it
everywhere then zoom around with rags on our feet. After that, we get
towels and throw them down to soak it all up. Voila! Clean floors.

The exciting news this week was Jalen losing another tooth (his
second) which he has proudly showed anyone who stands still long
enough, Ciara writing her first chapter for a book, Jared reaching
level 60 on World of Warcraft and Trevor going off to NC to help his
gf move into her college dorm.

OH, and Jalen's watermelons he is growing are getting BIG. Very
exciting!:)
