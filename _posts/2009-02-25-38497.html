QWERTYUIOP 

(A project founded and run by an 11 year old homeschooler with the help of her friends and family. Please share with your fellow home educators)




Visit  http://www.peacefrog.org/qwertyuiop/index.html




QWERTYUIOP is a literary magazine for home educated children ages 11 and up. Please show your support for this project by submitting your work and by subscribing to the magazine. Deadline for submissions for our next issue is March 15, 2009. Issue will be available April 1st, 2009.  QWERTYUIOP is a way for teens to share their writing, their artistic work and their creative self with peers. It is a way to have your work published, read and seen and to see the vision and creative brilliance of other fellow teens.

Editor: Jadziah Aphrodite Moonstone
