I'm in Denver. I've lived here for 10 years, but strangely enough I've
only been to Boulder a handful of times.

Boulder has lots of open space and many hiking trails. I don't know
specifics, but it should be easy to figure out just by asking around.

Pearl Street Mall is a pedestrian mall that's fun for people watching.
Fun shops, bookstores, and cafes.

Celestial Seasonings is nearby. They have a free tour that runs every
half hour to hour or so if you're interested in sampling some teas and
seeing how they're bagged and packaged.

I have too many things going on during this week already, otherwise I
would drive up to meet you. Have a fun visit!
