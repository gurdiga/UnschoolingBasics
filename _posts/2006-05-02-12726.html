I know that there are a lot of accidents on trampolines but we had one
for eight years and never had one accident on it. Our guidelines were
that only two children be on it at one time and they had to be similar
size so that one wasn't likely to bounce the other one off. My kids
spent HOURS on that thing and they played on it, not just bounced.
They created all kind of games that didn't require much bouncing.

I would suggest like someone else suggested that if it was possible
you might go with him. Great way to introduce yourself to the
neighbors!
