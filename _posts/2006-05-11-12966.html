Barbara has a new book out, Refuse to Choose! A Revolutionary Program for
Doing Everything That You Love. I haven't read all of it, but so far it feels
like an Unschooler's Bible. She describes what she calls Scanners -- people
who have many interests and passions.

I think Barbara has a handle on adult unschoolers who weren't raised
unschoolers. One of the things I love about her is her emphasis on your NOT having
to become "a better person" before you can have your dreams.

She's very cool.
