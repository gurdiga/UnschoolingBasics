check out wickedsmallgames.com. There's also one called whizzball on
the kids.discovery website - you build a "mousetrap" type thing with
springs and pushers and conveyors and such to get from the drop tube
to the 'goal' OR you can play a puzzle someone else has built - it
shows the start and the goal and gray areas where pieces go and an
inventory of which pieces to use for this particular puzzle. DH
likes trying to figure out puzzles, DS (8) likes to play with making
his own stuff.
