Radiotalk  Podcast will be featuring a nationwide radio show on "Unschooling",  LIVE from the 2009 UWWG on Wednesday, Feb. 3, noon - 1  EST.
 
http://www.blogtalkradio.com/parenting/2009/02/04/Unschooling-Live-at-UWWG
 
Lisa Stroyan and Amy Makice will be hosting their weekly radio talk show, LIVE from the 2009 UWWG ( Unschoolers Winter Waterpark Gathering)  in Sandusky, Ohio.   
 
Ruth Radney will be joining them to talk about  "Unschooling -  What it's all about and dispel some of the myths"!!!.  Other UWWG conference attendees will share their thoughts as well, along with call-ins from around the country! 
 
So, be sure to mark this date down and join in the LIVE radio Podcast!  It will be available thereafter, on the radiotalk website, for anyone to listen and download (can also be downloaded to MP3 and i-Tunes).
