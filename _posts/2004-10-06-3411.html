Hi guys. When I first came on this list, stating that I thought TV was evil (which I definitely no longer believe; thanks ya'll) and that we would never get cable, Rue (I think it was Rue) asked me "well, what if he wants cable?"
So, let me share this incident that happened today. We were at Wal-Mart and Will saw a display of TVs. He said "Buy me one of those TVs." I said, "we already have a TV!" and he replied, "No, I want a TV for my room." I said, "Okay, well mama doesn't have enough money to buy a new TV, but would you like to move the TV we already have into your playroom?" He said yes and we did just that as soon as we got home. He turned it on, started turning the channels and got MAD, and screamed, "I DON'T WANT THIS TV: I WANT AN ANIMAL PLANET TV!" So, obviously the child wants cable! We are moving in a couple of weeks and I am hoping this will make the move better for him...I called today and ordered DirectTV at our new home. It will be already hooked up when we move in.
Just wanted to share.
Peace and all good,
Millie

OT, but...

When we first got DirecTV (so dh could watch Philly Eagles games here
in DC!), it went out whenever it rained even a little. I was
disgusted at how much money we were paying for something that worked
so poorly! They weren't any help, saying that, yeh, it goes out when
it rains. Well, then fall came and the leaves fell off all the trees
in the neighborhood and presto! Then the service only went out when
it poured. So in spring, when we started having problems again (due,
apparently, to leaves getting heavy with rain, bringing the branches
down in the way of our dish), dh stood outside in the rain and moved
the dish until it started working again.

Anywho, this might help someone, somewhere...
