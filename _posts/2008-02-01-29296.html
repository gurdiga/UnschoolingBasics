Hi Everyone,


I am new to the group and thought I would introduce myself and my
almost 9-year-old daughter Hannah.

We began our unschooling adventure on Monday! So much has
unfolded for us already. Weeeeeeeeeeeeeee!

I've been reading everything I can and I have been enjoying the wisdom and
experience that is being shared in this group.

I also started a blog yesterday called "In Her Footsteps" to document our
adventure and just published my second post. My first post tells a little
about Hannah and my story.

If you would like to read it you can go to
http://stardustjoy.blogspot.com/

I'd love to hear your comments if you have the time or inclination.

I also want to thank Ren for her words suggestion to check out Thing A Day.
We've signed up. What fun!

I have another daughter, Madeleine, who is 11, and has chosen to
remain in public school. I deeply honor her choice and have left the
door open to alternatives.

Thanks again and we're happy to be here!

Best,
Nancy and Hannah
