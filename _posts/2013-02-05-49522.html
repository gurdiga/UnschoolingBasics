It's basically the same in Texas. All students take the Accuplacer
(used to be THEA). It covers math, reading and writing. Everyone takes it
unless they score over a certain number on their TAKS tests, Texas'
standardized testing given all through Elementary-High school. (have to be
passed to graduate)

My older kids all graduated or got their GED, which was required for
admittance.
