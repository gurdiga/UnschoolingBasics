Naomi Aldort will be hosting a telecall next Sunday (Jan 18) on
Unschooling. It's actually a two part call - the first 45 minutes will be
one-on-one with a woman (who is currently unschooling her 2-year-old son)
dealing with feelings of anger toward her son; the second 45 minutes will be
a one-on-one about unschooling. The unschooling one will actually be with
the husband (who has doubts about unschooling as do many husbands I know!)
and may or may not include the wife on the call as well.

Information is here:
http://www.naomialdort.com/phone2.html

The call is Jan 18 at 11AM PST and costs $20. You will be able to listen to
a recording after the fact if you sign up and are unable to listen live.

Feel free to pass the information along to any other parents who might be
interested.

Thanks!
