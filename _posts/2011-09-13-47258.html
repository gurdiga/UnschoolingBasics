Hi Kimmie, I'm Meredith. I have one "always unschooled" daughter who is 10 and a stepson who will be 18 in two weeks! Wooo Hoo! He's gone through some homeschooling and then school and finally unschooling so I've had a chance to see just how much unschooling can benefit a kid who has had a rough ride through traditional education and parenting. I soooo wish we'd started sooner!

Anyway, if you haven't found them yet, here are some good sources of information about whole life unschooling:

http://joyfullyrejoycing.com/

a smaller and more general unschooling site:
