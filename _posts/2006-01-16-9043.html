Just a short intro of me and mine. I'm Seana (See na), wife to the
greatest husband in the world Rob (I know we all think our own is
<grin>). We've been working at AP for about 4 years now.

My daughters are Genevieve, just turned 4 in Dec, and Elissa, will be
3 in April. About a year ago DH and I decided that maybe we should
think about schooling for our girls. Then after looking at PS and
ruling it OUT, wishing for Montessori and our bank account ruling it
out, we looked at homeschooling. Now I've come on to the unschooling
idea and it looks good.

So here I am, looking for ideas on how to make our lives full of fun
and learning. Mostly, I think what I need is energy. I'm 41 and
starting to feel it. Thanks in advance for all your support and
knowledge.
