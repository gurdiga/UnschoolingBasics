" One has two much pepper or another too much
sweetness. "

That's why making it yourself is so great!

3 tsp. cardoman chunks (I like green card. pods best, but harder to
find...use a bit more if using the green pods)
2tsp. whole cloves
1 tsp. ground or fresh ginger (use a bit more if using fresh)
5-6 whole allspice
a few peppercorns (if you don't want the spiciness, just leave them off)
5-6 cinnamon sticks
4cups water
4 cups whole milk (I use organic)
1/2 cup sugar (or more to taste)

I've used black tea in the past, but most of my friends like it better
without. So we usually don't use it.

Bring all spices and water to a boil, cover, lower heat and simmer for
20 minutes. Add milk and sugar, bring heat back up just until it's hot
(do not boil). Strain and serve.
