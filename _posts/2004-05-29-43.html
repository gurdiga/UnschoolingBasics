If he likes off the wall comics, this is right up his alley. It's
Japanese comics, and if you find authentic type publications, they
are actually backwards to us (start from back of book, read right to
left) the way real Japanese Manga would be...only it's translated to
English.
There are a ton of books showing people how to draw manga now...very
popular. You could find them at any Barnes and Noble if he's
interested.
