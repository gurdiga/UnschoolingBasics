There seem to be a bunch of new voices all of a sudden, so I wanted to be sure to point out the following wonderful resources about radical unschooling:

http://joyfullyrejoycing.com/
Joyce's comments are always very sensible and logical!

http://sandradodd.com/unschooling
A biiiig site with collected comments from many people over many years.

http://enjoylifeunschooling.com/
A collection of unschoolers blogs and other resources also hosting a "blog carnival" - blog posts written to a specific topic. Includes a list of unschooling gatherings and conferences on the main page -very handy!

http://sites.google.com/site/dragonflykaizen/unschooling-1
Collected posts from the Blog of the Zombie Princess on unschooling - great stuff, especially about teens. I highly recommend it!
