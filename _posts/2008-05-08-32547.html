Sarah

Hi there

Are you in a state/place that requires lots of paperwork and reporting
and all? If so, then maybe something like Clonlara would be helpful to
get started. I know a couple of unschoolers in fairly aggressively
hostile type places (places with few homeschoolers at all and fairly
restrictive laws) that use it and haven't had any problems. They seem
fairly laid back and willing to work *with* the direction you're taking
rather than forcing you into a set mold. But, if you have a fairly laid
back legal environment, WHY spend the money on it when you can use that
money to have FUN with the family?
