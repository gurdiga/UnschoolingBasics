***I'm using chocolate as my
example...***

My lovely Welsh Auntie, Eula, used to tell me there was a difference
between hot cocoa and hot chocolate. Hot chocolate she said, was never
made from cocoa powder. She always had different chocolates from around
the world and she made the loveliest hot chocolate, with real chocolate
and sometimes other ingredients (pepper, cinnamon, ground nuts...) and
told me (as Ren has discovered) If you know chocolate you're on your way
to understanding the secrets of the universe. : )
