Hello again,

we have a second request:

We are in search of families who can just shortly write statements
about practical stuff: what do they do with their toddlers and
preschoolers all day? Tell us your "best-ofs": Favourite games, fun
outings, interesting interests of your children that you think you
only found out about because you value unschooling principles and
their interests (like a child interested in spiders and bugs,
"traditionally parenting" parents would maybe have said, that's
"yuck!", but an unschooling parent helped their preschooler and now
they have a terrarium or something like that - just to give you an
idea what we're looking for.

It's not necessary, but you may also draw the line to how this or that
activity is "educational" from a schoolish point of view. But we don't
put too much focus on that, so just if it fits. It shouldn't sound
like unschooling parents take care that their children "learn" typical
educational subjects that are taught in school, just by playing.

It shouldn't be too lon, 100-400 words is enough! It can be short
statements. Just send them to

johanna.gundermann @ unerzogen-magazine.de

Please help us bring the unschooling idea forward in Germany!!! Not
even Homeschooling is allowed here!

Each author of the printed statements will of course get a copy of the
issue (in German though ;-)) via post mail, unless you don't want
that :-)

Greetings
Johanna
