I have the hotel information for the 2005 Live and Learn Unschooling
Conference.

We will be just across the river from St Louis---actually in Illinois.

We'll be at the Four Points by Sheraton in Fairview Heights, IL. You can
check out the hotel at _http://www.starwoodhotels.com/fourpoints/index.html_
(http://www.starwoodhotels.com/fourpoints/index.html)

The Central Reservation number is 1-800-325-3535.
If you'd like to call the hotel directly: 1-618-622-9500.

Be sure to mention the Live and Learn Unschooling Conference in Fairview
Heights, IL to get our low, low conference rate of $80/night. There are a very
few suites at $110/night.

If you're planning to come a few days early or stay late, you can get the
conference rate extended by asking.

The conference will be FOUR days this year, due to popular demand! <G> So
we'll be starting on THURSDAY morning instead of Friday and going through
SUNDAY---so October 6th through the 9th, 2005.

I met with our web designer on yesterday to iron out the website, so that
should be up very soon. I'll post when it's up and running.

Looking forward to seeing you all there! And please feel free to cross-post
at other unschooling e-lists/sites.

~Kelly
