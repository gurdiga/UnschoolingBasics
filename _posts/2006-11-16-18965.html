I haven't posted in a while but I wanted to thank this group for the support I was given when I decided to let my son unschool. He left school due to possible allergic reaction to chemicals or mold and I could immediately see that independent study or traditional homeschooling was not very attractive.

By reading here and educating myself I had the courage to allow him to take the proficience test this past October and he passed! It's such a relief to not be tied down to public school.

He spends most of his days sleeping late, on the computer and doing his music. He plays drums and has been teaching himself guitar, bass, things he may not otherwise have had time for.

It's been nice to have the extra time with him.
