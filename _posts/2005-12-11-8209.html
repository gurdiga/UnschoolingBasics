>>Hey got a question for coffee experts.. Rebecca & Mary. Does expresso
for lattes and mochas go through a filter usually or not? I am
curious.>>

The ground espresso goes into a small metal portafilter which fits directly under the hot water source. When the hot water is pulled, the espresso shot should take 18-24 seconds to complete. And, yes, a good barista will count their shots. ;-)

BTW a mocha is just a regular latte with chocolate syrup added (and maybe a little whipped cream.....yum).
