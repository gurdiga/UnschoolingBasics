Well, it's official, I will be speaking at the Great Big Happy Life
Conference next month. After lots of back and forth over the past
several months, I finally figured a way to work it out with the farm and
all, but I'll only be up there for Wednesday night, not the entire
conference.

Meredith Novak will also be speaking, and I know how many of you enjoy
her posts here and at AlwaysUnschooled. Cameron Lovejoy, too, will be
talking as a grown unschooler, which should be really inspiring. It
looks to be a great line up, especially that Gatto guy. ;)

Here's a link to the talk descriptions:
http://www.greatbighappylife.com/9943.html

Hope to see several of you there!
