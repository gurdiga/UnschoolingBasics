respectfully decline to answer informing the person asking that they really

data, real estate transactions, etc. He'd need to know where the birth
certificate was filed (where the child was born) and then check with
their town/county records office. There's usually a small processing fee
to get a duplicate birth certificate (or any other paperwork).
