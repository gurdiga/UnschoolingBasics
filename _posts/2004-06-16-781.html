Hi I'm Mila
I just join this group. I have 3 children, ages
11yrs. 9yrs. and 7 yrs old. We've been homeschooling
for 3 years now. And I'm just figuring out what I'm
doing. I for 2 years brought the classroom into the
home and it was horrible. It was like pulling teeth
out for all of us. And it was stressful. Finally I got
better info and started to really study about
homeschooling and found out there is so much out
there. The unschooling approach is more what I'm
looking for. I've let things go finally this year and
just let the kids lead me and to my surprise my kids
have come to me with ideas and we just go from there.
It feels like we're actually learning things and
injoying what we're learning and nobody is protesting
anymore. Is this unschooling? My oldest really freaked
out at first because she's used to text books, and my
teacher says this, or my teacher says that. And I tell
her I am her teacher and that we're all learning
together. Well it is working.
I hope this is the right group to be a part of.


Thanks for reading this,
Mila
