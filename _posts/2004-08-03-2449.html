Hi fellow unschoolers

Last week I was given this list address and told to join to also experience
the joy of communicating with like-minded people on the questions arising
from unschooling.

I'll try to keep the introduction short. I'm Ulrike and my youngest son
Alexander (12) made it very clear that he was not going to any normal school
or even special school. This was re-inforced when we went for the enrolment
talk at the local Waldorf school and the teacher told me afterwards that it
would not serve Alexander or the class if he were to join them. Since then
he (and I ) have been following our bliss at home.

We live in Windhoek, the Capital of Namibia - somewhere in Africa - and I
haven't met any other unschoolers here, yet.

Looking forward to great exchanges
Ulrike
