Hi Pam,

I tried twice to send this to your off-list email, and
both times it bounced back as undeliverable. I don't
know what the problem is -- the notices made no sense
to me.

Here is my state info:

New Mexico here - unschooling is very easy here, with
only a legal requirement that we register as
homeschoolers and the parent have a high school
diploma or equivalent. In my experience, the
requirement is largely un-enforced, having been late
in registering a couple of times. I know other
unschoolers here who never register, and never get
'found out.'

As to rating, probably a 9.5, maybe a 10. I understand
that in Oklahoma, there's not even a requirement to
register, and in Texas, one only has to establish a
private school at the home address and anything else
is okay from there. Then again, I have no desire to
live in either OK or TX and I love NM, so that makes
it
easily a 10 in my book!
