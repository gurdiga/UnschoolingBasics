On Feb 28, 2009, at 10:11 PM, Stephanie Mitchell wrote:

> Where can i find some information and interact with those who are
> sharing their every day lives, not just the theory side of
> things?

Blogs are a great place to start. Sandra has a short list and each of
those has links to their favorite blogs:
http://sandradodd.com/help

There are probably blogs at Radical Unschoolers Network:
http://familyrun.ning.com/profiles/blog/list

And most people have fancied up their profiles with information.

The practical stuff is often shared when people bring up a specific
problem.

Philosophy and practical work hand in hand. Philosophy is basically
what you test action against to make sure you're keeping with what
you value. The practical shows what the philosophy can look like when
put into action in a particular situation. They clarify each other.

Joyce
