If you'd like to, you can purchase cd's or mp3s or download many talks from previous conferences. I love to listen to conference recordings, especially on long car trips. My children often talk with me or ask questions about what is said as sometimes they listen too.

Life Is Good Conference Talks:
http://lifeisgoodconference.com/registration/products-page/presentation-recordings/

Live and Learn Conference Recordings:
Write to Kelly Lovejoy to see if she could put them on a cd for you. Here email address is kelly lovejoy kbcdlovejo@...

Unschoolers Emporium has conference talks from the Toronto Unschoolers Conferences, WeShine conferences, Life Is Good Conferences and others.
http://www.theunschoolersemporium.com/marketplace/categories/conference-talks/

Wide Sky Days (formerly Good Vibrations): Not sure how to order these yet. I wrote to Flo Gascon to ask.

This one is free and available online. Maybe it can be downloaded to a cd to take in the car?
2011 LTTL (London) Conference talks:
http://www.lttl.org.uk/download-sound-files.html
