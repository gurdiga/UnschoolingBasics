April

If you search Yahoo! Groups you will find several homeschool groups
in Michigan. I find that an easy way to find things to do. Just
join and check their calendars periodically. Here are the names of
the ones I belong to:

Michigan-Macomb County Homeschoolers

Macomb Christian Homeschoolers

Michigan Unschoolers

SCS Homeschool Support

SE Michigan Homeschooling Teens

There are unschoolers that belong to each of these groups, plus
there are people in your area on a few of them. If you do the
general search through the groups you should be able to find groups
in your area. I know that your specific area has active groups.

We are in Roseville, so we're no help as far as getting together.
Best of luck on your quest. Have fun...

Tina
