Dear Friends,

Well the campaign against Amazon is in full swing! Thanks to everyone
who wrote them last week, asking them to remove 4 baby-whipping books
from their website. Let's keep the pressure on!

Please call them at (800) 201-7575 within the US, or
(877) 586-3230 for Canada, or
0800 279 6629, United Kingdom.

Politely say "Please stop selling baby-whipping books. Making money by
selling books such as 'To Train Up a Child' by Pearl, 'Shepherding a
Child's Heart' by Tripp, 'What the Bible Says About Child Training' by
Fugate, and 'Child Training Tips' by Bradley is unethical and only
encourages blatant child abuse. All 4 of these books recommend hitting
infants under 1 year of age, with sticks and whips. Following the advice
in these books has resulted in serious charges of child abuse. Until you
stop selling these child abuse manuals, I will not purchase items from
Amazon."

You can also complain about these books by writing a comment in the box
at the bottom of this page (scroll down to *Comments or Examples* ):

Also, please write a review of "What the Bible Says About Child
Training" if you haven't yet
http://www.stoptherod.net/biblical-child-training-excerpt.html

Thank you everyone, for speaking out against these outrageous books!!
