My sister-in-law, the pediatric oncology nurse, recommended this to me
when I asked about Hannah Jenner's meds being so awful:

Get Skittles and warm them in your hands. When they are soft enough to
flatten, then put the meds inside and wrap the flattened Skittle around
it. *Don't* do this surreptitiously! Let her know what you're doing so
that she doesn't bite down on it accidentally. But that way she can eat
a few, swallow one whole, and then chew a few more.

Good luck!
