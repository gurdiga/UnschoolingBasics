That's a great way to look at it...each day is a new day to do better,
to learn and grow, just like our children.
Making them go to bed is a better option than yelling or hitting, and
next time things get tense, maybe the option will be a distraction, or
a drive or something nicer than going to bed.:)

It's easy to blame the unschooling, after all, we're in uncharted
territory...it can feel uneasy to re-write the book on parenting that
we were handed as children. But we're doing it. One day, one moment at
a time, the shift happens.
