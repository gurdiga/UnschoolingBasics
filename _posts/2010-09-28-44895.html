For FL, whether you currently have your Home Education Program registered with your county or you have your children enrolled in an umbrella school, there is nothing different, legally, that you need to do in order to live as unschoolers.

If you have the annual evaluations under the county method, you will need to find an evaluator who gets unschooling.

If you are using the umbrella option, you need to find one that fits with unschooling.

Write me off-list if you want more info.
