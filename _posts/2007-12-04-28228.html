> Do you have a daughter that might be interested in having an e-mail pen pal?

My daughter (8) joined the new yahoo group Anne Ohman created for kids:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/weareunschoolers/

She says there is lots of chatting going on between kids of different
ages. She did the e-pal thing for a while but it didn't really take
off like she hoped. She told me this is easier because it's lots of
kids to talk with instead of one person and everyone can share the
things they like with everyone else. I think she really likes that
it's other unschoolers. She can talk freely about video games and
Harry Potter, lol. Maybe your daughter would enjoy it, too?

~ Susan
