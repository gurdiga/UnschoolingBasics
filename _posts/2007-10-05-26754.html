?great source for black & white illustrations is Clipart ETC. The copyright policy allows up to 50 illustrations in a single educational project. They have some nice "period" illustrations, as well as plenty of pictures of historical figures. I find these are helpful to include illustrations with greyscale texts, for making colouring sheets, lapbooks, timelines, etc.

To search their database, go to http://etc.usf.edu/clipart/mySearch.php. This is a resource provided by the University of South Florida
