aroundthemilkyway

8 May 2006, at 1:24am

realized this... when i wake up in the morning i need to ask the girls
what they want to do with the day rather than thinking about what i
need to accomplish....now i will be able to accomplish "this or that"
as they will often desire to play with dolls, do a puzzle, watch a
movie, ride scooters, or play a computer game just by themselves in
which case i can work on the things i would like to accomplish....BUT
realizing that not everyday will i get that done and it is 'learning
all the time' to put them first and play, explore, read, scoot, watch
or dig *with* them if and when they desire that!!

i know....duh, but i have had the tendency to "get my stuff done
first" and then....blah blah blah.

yet another little leaf turned over in this freedom learning journey.
