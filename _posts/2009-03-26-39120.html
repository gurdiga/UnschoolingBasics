We may be moving to rural Southwestern Wisconsin in six months to a year. My inlaws have purchased an 1880's era hotel/bed and breakfast with a restaurant and will move out in June to start the restoration process. We hope to follow when we figure out what to do for income there. (I had planned to apply for graduate programs in Madison, but I'm not sure I can do that while unschooling.)
