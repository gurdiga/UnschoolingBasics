I know we've been talking a lot about getting our homes in order and
chores. Here is a link to the Holidays Control Journal from Flylady.
Basically it is a 25 page document helping you to get ready for the
holidays. It will guide you in all areas--cleaning your home, buying
gifts, decorating for the holidays, etc.

Oh, I love the Holiday Control Journal!! I never got into the habit
of using her regular Control Journal (although I *do* have what I call
my Great Big Book of Everything and the whole family knows that's
where all the really important things are), but I have been using the
Holiday one for several years now. It really helps me keep track of
things, like when I buy presents a few months early. December rolls
around and I have no idea what I did in September. LOL

I put my HCJ in a red folder/binder thingy and decorated it with fancy
Christmas stickers. :-)
