We do Brikwars!!! We're even putting the finishing touches on an article
about it for a future issue of the Connections ezine. DH and DS (8 1/2)
love it. If you download the BrikWars instructions, the first section is
a 'quick start' where it's a very basic gameplay and then you can go
from there. My guys adjust and modify and add and make up all sorts of
new "rules" to accommodate whatever pieces we have/can build (since we
might not have A but we have B so we adjust for that).
