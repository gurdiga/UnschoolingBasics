hi all

when i started unschooling i had a support group that
met once a month in the evening for parents and
nursing babies only. it was great to see people face
to face and also to put a face with an email. If
people are interested in a get together on a monthly
basis I would be willing to volunteer my home. i am
in northboro ma right off 290. I am close to 495 and
about 15 min from mass pike. for those of you who
know the area i live about 5minutes from the solomon
pond mall.

we used to have topics like "a day in the life of my
family", nintendo, dealing with siblings, time for
yourself etc. but it was mostly a come with questions
and lets all throw it around.

let me know if anyone is interested.

love and peace
dina
