It's sort of on topic because it's related to Our Unschooled Life.

gift for each other, then we open them Christmas eve. It's sometimes harder as
Julian gets older to think of something, but I was SOO happy with what I made
for him, and he loved it, so I wanted to share.

_http://www.fat-pie.com/salad.htm_ (http://www.fat-pie.com/salad.htm)

Anyway, I made Julian a Salad Fingers doll on the knitting looms. The
picture is on my knitting loom lens here: _http://www.squidoo.com/loomknit/_
(http://www.squidoo.com/loomknit/) It's with the other pictures near the bottom.

shawl, and made Julian a collage about the marriage equality fight that he
worked so hard for.

It was a really nice Christmas eve.
