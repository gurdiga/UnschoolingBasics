HOMESCHOOL PEN-PALS
CHILDREN AND parents ALIKE CAN JOIN

This is a group for homeschoolers and their parents to sign up for pen
pals for all over the world!
Please post a note of introduction for yourself and/or child in our
messages and then sign up in the database with your name, age of the
person who wants a pen pal, and your email address.You can chose a pen
pal from these listed and emailthem to see if they want to exchange or
wait to see if someone picks your info from here. You can also ask on
the messages for a specific type of pen pal and folks can answer you
by email from them.
