UPCOMING TRIPS

New York City - May and June
Tour New York City with us and visit the Statue of Liberty, Ellis
Island, Times Square, see a Broadway play, walk the Brooklyn
Bridge, play in Central Park, and more.
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelnyc.htm

Space Camp - September
Join us for an exploration of space, astronomy, and technology at
Space Camp! During our 3-day field trip, we'll experience training
simulators, complete a Space Shuttle mission, learn about space
history, and see first-hand what it takes to become an astronaut.
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelspa.htm

Washington DC - September and October
We're going back to Washington DC this fall to visit the memorials
and monuments, White House, Arlington Cemetery, US Capitol,
Ford's Theater, your choice of museums, and more.
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelwdc.htm

Disney World - November
Travel with us on our 6th trip to Disney World and explore the
parks, play virtual reality games at Disney Quest, participate in
special behind-the-scenes programs - all while enjoying the
wonderful Christmas decorations and festivities. Join us!
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelwdw.htm
