Hello Dads!!

May 1, 2 &3 in the mountains near Johnson City, TN we are hosting our
1st Dads' Weekend. What is a Dads' Weekend? Well we're not really
sure. The Dads in NE TN had an idea to rent a cabin in the
beautiful mountains of TN and just hang out, talk about being an
unschooling Dad, how it's going, maybe some drinking, card games,
cycling, nerf gun tag (Scotty's fave)…whatever we all wanted to do.
As the word spread, more and more Dads showed an interest so we
decided to invite all Dads.

Accommodations:
Each cabin has 1 queen and 2 twin beds. With blow-up beds on the
living room floor the cabins sleep 4-6. Each cabin has a full
kitchen with plates, cups, etc and costs $114 a night. (Divided by 4
not bad) There are no TVs or phones. Wireless internet is a ten
minute walk to the main building. You will need to bring all your
own food and drink for the weekend. The closest town in 20 minutes
away.

Roan Mountain
http://www.tennessee.gov/environment/parks/RoanMtn/

If you are interested email me directly at Austin@...

Please pass this email on to every list of unschooling folks.

Best,

Austin Gavin
