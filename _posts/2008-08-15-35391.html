homeschooling family in need of blood - AB - with an IgA


kindly forward

A friend of mine's little boy is very sick right now and the family
is in need of prayers. LOTS of them.

However, there is a more urgent need at this time. He needs a blood
transfusion and because of his illness and his blood type finding a
donor is a 1 in a million shot.

He is AB - with an IgA (immunoglobulin) deficiency

If you or anyone you know has this rare blood type and deficiency and
are willing to donate blood to Aaron, please contact me so I can get
you in touch with the family.

Aaron's caring bridge site --
http://www.caringbridge.org/visit/aaronczup
