Today, I learned of an awesome exhibit in Hartford, Connecticut at
the XL Center. I have no marketing materials to post, so please bear
with me. I do not know if this is a traveling exhibit that will come
to your state or not.

From April 12- the end of July or August, the XL Center in Hartford
has the Titanic Exhibit.

I guess this is not just a bunch of artifacts on shelves...but they
have the exhibit set up where your ticket is a boarding pass with the
name of an actual passenger on it. When you enter, you are walking
on boards like that from a ship in that time. There are passage ways
that make you feel like you are actually on the Titanic!

The exhibit is interactive and I thought it would be an awesome
history lesson for home schoolers.

I plan on traveling from Western New York State to Hartford next
month on business. I also plan on bringing my kids so we can check
it out in person.

Have a great day!
