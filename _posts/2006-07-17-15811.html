Speaking of back to school supplies, don't you just love it when they
put the crayons and markers and pencils and paper and notebook really
cheap? We pick up a few things during the big sales, but we try to
hold off until the second week after school starts when they mark
everything down even further. I jumped on Crayola markers being on
sale for 88¢ this week at Target! I also bought a 10 pack of spiral
notebooks as the kids go through those things like crazy with notes,
plays, drawings, games, doodling, crumpled ball cat toys :-) When I
came in the door with this huge bag of "school supplies" Mary Elayne
(almost 11) decided to become melodramatic screaming, "NO NO NO YOU
WON'T SEND ME BACK TO SCHOOL! I'D RATHER DIE FIRST! I'M DYING! I'M
DYING! I'M DYING!" ROFL. Then today we were at the grocery store and
there was a display of lunch boxes and she said, "Mommy, save me from
the school lunch boxes. Please tell me you aren't going to buy one
and send me to school!" I laughed and said, "Why would I want to send
you to school? No fear there!" Of course that got some very odd
looks from the stockboy ;)
