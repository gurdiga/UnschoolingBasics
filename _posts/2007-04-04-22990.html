passions and I would like to share. The Bach Flower essences and
Flower Essence Society North American Flower Essences are a system of
natural remedies for various emotional imbalances such as fear, grief,
anger etc. They are similar to homeopathy but not the same.They won't
interact with standard meds and have no side effects so are great for
children.
I have been using them for 11 years now and studying them in depth for
about five years. When I don't have a little nursing baby I plan to go
and complete the Professional training to be a practitioner!
I have read a lot of posts describing anxiety and fears in some
children,from concerns about environment to social fears and diagnosed
anxiety disorders. If any of you would like to use the flower
essences, want to know more specific ones that help, feel free to
ask,on or off group. No charge of course, just something that has been
profoundly helpful for my family and i would like to share it! :)

Wild Rose helps with resignation and lack of joy in life.

Mustard if he feels overwhelmed by a "black cloud" for unknown reasons

Gorse for discouragement, darkness hopelessness or resignation

Wild Oat for dissatisfaction with ones work, despair over finding life's
