There are tons of unschooling blogs out there - in my experience
though, most of them have lots of family stuff on them since
*everything* falls under unschooling. But it's nice to see the
perspective of unschoolers. Go here http://www.ringsurf.com/netring?
ring=Unschooling_Blogs;action=list and you will find a list of links
to some unschooling blogs. Also, some other ones I like are:
http://radicalunschooling.blogspot.com/,
http://www.findingme.typepad.com/, http://no-assembly-
required.blogspot.com/, http://www.sandradodd.blogspot.com/ ... there
are tons more, too many to name! If you start one, make sure to let
us know so we can check it out!
