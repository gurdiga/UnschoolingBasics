Hi - I started a group about a year ago that is very active. It is in
Bergen NJ/ Rockland NY and is for Attachment Parents who are homeschooling. There
are a lot of us who are unschoolers, and we will eventually have meetings
just for the unschoolers. If you AP, please feel free to join The Learning
Community (TLC) at _http://groups.yahoo.com/group/TLC_Bergen_Rockland/_
(http://groups.yahoo.com/group/TLC_Bergen_Rockland/) Everyone is very accepting of
unschoolers in TLC.

For now, we go to the unschooler meetings through Tri-County Unschoolers.
That is usually at Bear Mountain. Their yahoo group is
_http://groups.yahoo.com/group/tricounty_unschoolers/?yguid=89238718_
(http://groups.yahoo.com/group/tricounty_unschoolers/?yguid=89238718)

Good luck!

Unschoolily yours,
Cynthia
