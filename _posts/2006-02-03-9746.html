HOW TO ENTER
NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. Enter by printing your name, date of
birth, your parent's/guardian's name if you are under 18, full address,
phone number and how you heard about this contest on either an 8.5" x 11"
piece of paper or an online printout entry at
www.randomhouse.com/teens/youngwarriors and include a picture of yourself as
a young warrior and in 250 words or less tell us why you are one. Mail to
Young Warriors Contest, Random House Children's Books, Marketing Department,
1745 Broadway, 10-2, New York, NY 10019. Entries must be mailed separately
and received by Random House by January 31, 2006. LIMIT ONE ENTRY PER
FAMILY. Partially completed or illegible entries will not be accepted.
Sponsors will not be responsible for lost, late, mutilated, illegible,
stolen, postage-due, incomplete or misdirected entries. All entries and
photos become the property of Random House and will not be returned, so
please keep a copy for your records.

ELIGIBILITY
Contest is open to legal residents of the fifty (50) United States and the
District of Columbia, and Canada, excluding the Province of Quebec, who are
between the ages of 8 and 25 on October 11, 2005. All federal, state, and
local laws and regulations apply. Void wherever prohibited or restricted by
law. Employees of Random House Inc. and their parent companies, assigns,
subsidiaries or affiliates, and advertising, promotion, and fulfillment
agencies; their immediate family members, and persons living in their
household are not eligible to enter this contest.
