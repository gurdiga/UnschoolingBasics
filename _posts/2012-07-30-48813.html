Registration is now open for the 6th Annual Unschooler's Waterpark Gathering at the Kalahari Resort in Sandusky, Ohio. Our gatherings are for unschooling families from around the country to meet up as a community, share their experiences, have fun, relax and enjoy the waterpark together! Registration is only $35 per family. Please check out the website for information about speakers, funshops, main events and more! www.unschoolerswaterparkgathering.com The Kalahari Resort also offers an earlybird registration package consisting of a $20 gift card, $20 arcade card and mini golf passes for registering before October 1st.

Hope to see you there!

Kim Lowery
UWG Main Events Coordinator
