Midwifery Bill Introduced -
The Political Climate in Oklahoma has recently changed. We know that
Midwives and Parents

their rights by making

join the free on-line group.

available for your clients/friends.
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/FriendsofOkMidwivesAlliance/ contact to join!

type in your 9 digit zip code.
AGAIN - Feb 6, 2006 the calls must begin.
