Hi Folks,

Someone had written on another list I belong to:

>There is a homeschooling working parent yahoo group - not specific
>unschooling - so my understanding is that there is much talk about
>getting school work done etc.

So, I decided to change! The new group is called WRU - Working
Radical Unschoolers. I just started a new group specifically for
folks who work and radically unschool their younguns. Here's the
partial list description:

"This list description is under construction for now, but this is a
group specifically for Radical Unschooling moms and dads who work,
either part time or full time, outside the home or inside the home.
The intention in creating this list is to hold a space for people who
radically unschool and work (or have worked in the past while
unschooling or see this coming up in the future). That can be tricky
to do, and while I have found resources for homeschoolers who work, I
have yet to find a discussion group for radical unschoolers who work.
So, I'm creating one!"

And, here's the address for folks interested in joining:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/WRU/

Please feel free to pass this on!

Blessings,
Marji, who doesn't have enough to do!
