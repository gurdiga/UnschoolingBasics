Hello all.

Over the past five years of following my heart and intuition here on
Vancouver island to Unschool my children on every level, this is what
i have discovered. This has been a journey deep into self and looking
at all the beliefs i personally have. Children are naturals at this,
this is there innate state. There is nothing to defend against there
is nothing to make special about it, or to make any other belief
system right or wrong. It really is a journey of trust. I have come to
know this has been part of my spiritual path. I think the most
important thing, is whether a child is in school, or if they are
not...its about all of us following our hearts and our inner guidance
in whatever form that make take.

I personally feel that unschooling is more than unschooling.As my
children lead the way as what is natural to them, i can trust and look
at any all fears that arise in myself or others around me, knowing its
an opportunity just to look into my own mind to see what my beliefs
are..

Just felt to share...Sandy
