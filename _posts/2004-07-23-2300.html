23 Jul 2004, at 4:10pm

Dana:

Here in Florida, and homeschooling parent may start their own 600 school, which I assume is the same as an umbrella school. One of the mom's in my homeschool group started and registered her own, and the law says, as far as I understand, that it can not be set up just for your own children. So she has to have other kids enrolled in the school. The school is not accredited, there is no testing or school work of any kind. The mom also is an unschooler. Also, by having him enrolled in "school" I do not have to have my son evaluated annually, per the local laws. HTH

Michelle in FL
