Hello :)

We are happily RUNing with our 13yo ds in the Treasure Valley Area. We
are not really new to unschooling at all but being that I have a teen,
younger/new families are often looking to us for advice. I love this
list because there are so many great ideas for new/transitioning
families, it really helps me to remember what it was like for us and
understand what new families are going through :)

If any of you are from our area and want to visit with other local
unschoolers we just started a new group and a collaborative web project
that is being primarily developed by the kids. Come & see us and help
us make them into something great :)

Thanks!
Chris in Boise
