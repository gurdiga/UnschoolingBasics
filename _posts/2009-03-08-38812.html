What does he like to do? Does he want responsibilities? What does that mean to him? Babysitting? Or working under the table for a landscaper or a craftsperson, maybe? I don't know anything about your area, but is there agricultural work available?

Is he simply bored and looking for new directions? I'm always a little leery when a parent new to an unschooling list says either "real world" or "responsibilities" - its often about the parent's agenda moreso than the kid's. Radical unschooling happens in the real world, already. There are real problems to solve daily - even problems like "what am I going to do today?" (that's time management).
