This is a bit OT but I just want to spread the word. Imagination Tribe
is a group I created a few years ago for encouraging creative
expression and hosting art trades. We've recently started a "Ning"
group and I'm trying to inspire people to start local groups in
various areas. This is not a group strictly for unschoolers, anyone
wanting to create and trade art is welcome.

So far, we have local Imagination Tribes started in Chicago,
DC/Baltimore, East TN, Central CA and Southern CA. If anyone is
interested in checking out the local "tribes" or learning more about
IT, come on over: http://imaginationtribe.ning.com/
