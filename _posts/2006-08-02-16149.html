I had my nose pierced when I was much younger. I was 16 when I pierced it (
myself OUCH) but my sister at 36 got hers pierced professionally and she
said it was like a little bee sting. Well I am MISS ALLERGY LOL I am pretty
much allergic to anything environmental so during spring summer and fall I have
a hanky permanently attached to my nose. The jewelry I always used had
pretty short posts, so they didn't really stick out in your nostril much. But
when blowing you do have to be mindful that there is a big metal thing that can
hurt you if you press to hard. It took me only one time of forgetting to
never do it again LOL.
