I also want to comment on this issue...

this is from my own experience now seeing how unschooling is not only
liberating to the children but to the parents as well... I have mentioned
before about my struggle with food addiction... now that I've let up on all
food limitations (for myself also), I not only have stopped overeating but
no longer crave the foods that once controlled me. McDonald's makes me sick
to think about, I may have chocolate every now and then... because it is now
a non-issue... it's no longer controls my thoughts and actions.

I do know from listening to my body that I don't make the wisest choices
when I'm tired, so I am extra careful after work not to overdo it on the
food...

but I just want to get my voice out there on this issue because freedom is
really the best thing :)
