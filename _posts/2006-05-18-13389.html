Christy Putnam

18 May 2006, at 3:50pm

Great article Joanne! I must say that most of your article applies to all
older children, biological or not. there are a lot of things you mention
that compare to an older child that has spent a significant period of time
in the school system (mine was there for 7+years including pre-k & k) then
are removed to homeschool. My son was dxed adhd at age 5 and though I
refused to medicate and we just worked through it by trying to give him
tools to use throughout the day (and he had a supportive teacher that
followed up on him through his elem. time), we had a hard time really
bonding because of all the struggles at school and with homework. Since he
has been home and we have been implementing more unschooly ways, our bond is
getting much stronger. I would encourage anyone who is thinking of taking
their older child out of the school system to homeschool/unschool to take
the leap and do it even if just for a year or two...it is amazing how
quickly our lack of bonding is beign repaired (of course I would like to
have those years back so we didn't have to be repairing)! I am grateful
everyday of the fact I was introduced to unschooling. It has been such a
blessing!

thank you for sharing your article!

In Gratitude,
