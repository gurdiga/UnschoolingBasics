Hi my name is Cheryl. My family and I have been unschooling for years now. We moved back to Florida over a year ago after two years in California.

I've been part of a homeschooling eList in my county for about a year and recently I saw a post for an unschooling park day. The boys and I jumped at the chance to meet some other unschoolers in our area, but I quickly realized that I was the only radical unschooler in attendance. As the conversation progressed it became obvious to the other moms there as well! :)

There were people who were truly interested in unschooling so I made a yahoo group for the purpose of listing some of my favorite unschooling links, books, etc. Of course, this group is on there.

We are hoping to meet weekly at the park, and I'm hoping to make some good friends and be able to answer questions about unschooling as they come up. Being back on this list and reading along will help me as I tend to get a little tongue tied, so thanks for still being around after all these years!

Cheryl

ps- Hi to any old friends, we were at Live And Learn in St. Louis. We stayed in the hotel that was evacuated in the middle of the night for a false fire alarm! We were also at the Legoland meetup a few years back.
