RATS!!
Our rattie girls were well loved by all members of the family
(we've now lost all but one of them to old age). They're so smart
and loving, just like a miniature dog or something.
With a wee little one, you do have to be very present while they're
handling them, but even 2y.o. children adore rats and can cuddle and
hold them.
Jalen holds our last little rattie girl all the time. The only
problem is when he leaves her in his bed or in the clean laundry!!
So sweet, but not to be trusted on the chewing or pooping issues.:)
