8) Remember that we can ask for "do overs," "rewinds," and "try
agains." Our children need to hear us apologize and do better when we
screw up---and they're very generous with the number of times they'll
let us fix ourselves.

9) At the same time, we don't GET a "do over." Childhood is NOT a dress
rehearsal. This is the ONE chance we get with each child. We can't do
the whole childhood over once it's over. We only get this one shot.

Often, we hear someone say that her parents are *much* better
grandparents than they ever were as parents---and she wishes they had
been that nice to *her* as a child.

What a waste! Be the grandparent NOW to your own children! <G>
