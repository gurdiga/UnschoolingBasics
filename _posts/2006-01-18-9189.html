Here is a link for the boomwhackers. There are different packages and
accessories that go with them. I bought mine at a local music store.

Basically, they are tubes that you hit on any other object to make
music and sounds. They are supposed to be tuned to sound like musical
notes. I think you can even get sheet music to play songs.
