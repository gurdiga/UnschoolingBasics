Gosh, it's hard to get cooler than Peabody, Massachusetts.

Well, okay... Peabody itself is not really the hotbed of excitement some
would expect. BUT:

* it's near Salem, a National Historic site, which has witch museums, a
pirate museum, the Peabody Essex Museum, great restaurants, a small fun amusement
park (The Willows), witchy shops, etc.
* it's near Boston, site of more excitement than you can probably stand
*It's near Gloucester, an amazing seaside city with whale watches, etc.

AND it's the site of the 3rd Annual Live and Learn Unschooling Conference,
where you can hang out with 342 (so far) unschooling folks. I mean...how cool is
that?

The Conference Czar
