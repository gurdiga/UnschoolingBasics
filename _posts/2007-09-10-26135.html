Sara

10 Sep 2007, at 8:55pm

As I don't have a car yet, we just mostly hang in the house. My
husband is mostly too busy to take us out. I got lots of colorful
papers to make crafts with them. But what? my son isn't interested at
them at all. at first he claimed he wants to make a butterfly but he
lost interest. He plays in the computer and watches movies of spiderman.

I make breakfast he comes to eat it then runs back to the computer.
I got a nice book so I read it. My son comes sits next to me asking me
to read it to him.

Then the boy from the friends who are also homeschooled is coming to
my house. His younger brother who is my son's age is going to school.
(he asked his mother to go to school)
Both of the boys run upstairs to the computer again.

I went downstairs to do housework. Then dinnertime came already. My
son finished to eat then he walks around singing to himself. or he is
riding the trycycle.

some days I take him out to the park with the younger siblings.
(sister and brother)

Well, i can't help it but be self conscious when the people are asking
me: what are you doing all day?!! It certainly look like I do
'nothing' with my son.

Is it okay? (Here I go, deschooling myself too!)
