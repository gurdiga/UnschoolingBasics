Hey all, a friend of mine has started her own message board regarding
non-violent living and peace activism. Her goal is for uncensored
discussion and respectful exchanges. She just opened the site
yesterday morning. Please consider suggesting it to anyone who might
be interested. The board is focused on "exploring and actively working
to create a world without fear and exploitation".



Here is the Welcome:

Quote:
<<<<<We are exploring and actively working to create a world without
fear and exploitation.

A world of peace doesn't include false decorum and platitudes when
people are suffering or oppressed. We are committed to making waves.
Peaceful, loving waves that change the world-powerful in their
kindness and compassion. There are a multitude of perspectives on how
to achieve peace. This is a place for sharing challenges and ideas
with others who share our ideals of a non-violent world. We can learn
from each other in an atmosphere of mutual respect for our common
goals. Our alternative points of view will demand debate and
stretching our mental and emotional boundaries. However, namecalling
and hateful exchanges are not welcome in our shared efforts for
creating peace. Peaceful exchanges will be encouraged and openly
supported. All concerns regarding hateful posts may be brought to the
attention of administration. All moderation involvement will be done
openly and without secrecy. Please feel welcome to contact
administration regarding your concerns at any time.

It is expected that people will not agree with all the ideas found
here. An open mind, tolerance for differences and a willingness to
explore and learn are strongly suggested as prerequisites to
participating here. Most members have experienced a paradigm shift in
thinking and that means that on the surface that our cultural context
will be a limitation to growth and understanding. If this is not the
place for you, please let us discuss our lives in peace.

Thank you for joining us in discussing the political, socio-economic,
religious, cultural and parenting beliefs associated with creating a
peaceful world together.>>>>

It has open registration. She hopes to attract intelligent discussion
and debate as a public forum. Please join and pass it on.
