16 Dec 2006, at 1:22pm

Just want to shout at how I am enjoying watching my children. I am a
new unschooler and have posted once. The first thing I have done is
rearranged our book shelves and made nothing off limits to my kids.
Here are the things they have done in the last two days on their own.

Mosaic pictures from a kit
Coloring books of Native americans
Paper dolls of Camelot
Made an art/history museum in their room
Put america at war cards in chronological order
Colored in a Medieval coloring book
excitement of president stickers

and many other things, it would be to long to list.

All these items were things I was saving for some kind of study I
was going to do. So unfortunatly they were off limits to the girls.
Shame on me.

They are right now as we speak chasing after eachother teasing about
who's president sticker is the best. We are all having the time of
our lives.

Dawn
