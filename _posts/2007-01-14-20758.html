This may be old news for some but we just discovered, while playing
around on the Lego website, under downloads there is a program - FREE -
that lets you build Lego creations on the screen. It is called Lego
Digital Designer. You can then see and print the instructions for your
completed project...you can even go further and send them to Lego and
receive, (after payment for the bricks) the complete package in the mail
with the pieces necessary and directions for your model! You can even
design the packaging!! How cool is that. Ethan and Claire were up till
midnight playing around with the features. You can also save and share
your creations in the online gallery.
Amazing...and FREE!!!

Have fun,
Amy
