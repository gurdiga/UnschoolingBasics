Just wanted to let you all know, that there is also a Flickr group for
unschoolers. Members can post up to three photos per day, of
unschooled kids/families pursuing life.

You have to create your own account and then join the group page.
http://flickr.com/groups/unschooling/

It's free and easy, and there are a ton of great photos there. I LOVE
looking through them...gives me inspiration.

Also, I'm trying to take people off moderation as much as possible, so
if you want to post something you aren't comfortable going public
with, please test out your membership to see if you're still on mod or
not. You can always email the list owners directly if you wish to post
something anonymously.
