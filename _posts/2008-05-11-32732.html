So...I've been writing a lot these last two days. <BWG>

I'm on a high. Literally, I WAS high yesterday.

13,000 feet.

And then I jumped out of a perfectly good airplane!

Ben said last year that he wanted to sky-dive. Being the loving wife
that I am, that's what I gave him for his birthday: a tandem trip out
of an airplane.

Of course, Cameron wanted to go too, so we added him. Then his
girlfriend, Carly, HAD to jump too, so we added her. Duncan wanted to,
but, at 12, he's too young. I wanted to as well; but because Dunc
wouldn't jump for six more years, I agreed to wait and jump with him
then.

But when yesterday came, Dunc didn't want to get up so early and drive
all the way there just to watch, so he stayed home and slept late.

Everyone was geared up and waiting---and there was an empty spot. So I
took it!

It's a l-o-n-g way down from 13,000 feet!

Being the birthday boy, Ben got the video. The rest of us will just
have to settle with our memories. <G>

We'll ALL go again in six years with Duncan. <g>

As for Dunc: he's leaving tomorrow morning for Atlanta to fly up to
Boston *all by himself* to stay with friends for a week until we get
there for the North East Unschooling Conference. He's very excited (he
won't sleep tonight, I know!) and I'm sure a little frightened. But the
excitement's definitely winning out! <g>

So I took a big step yesterday, and Duncan's taking a big one tomorrow!

~Kelly
