Hi,

I consulted my 12 year old son on this - he's an Xbox expert!

His advice:

Definitely get a second Xbox (easy for him to say and when we suggested
getting one for his 9 yr old brother who never gets a lookin he said it was
a waste of money!)

Get the 2 younger boys to play multi player games together on the 2nd Xbox

He believes there will only be an overheating problem if it's left on for 40
hrs - he often plays his all daylong and we have not had the ring of fire
problem
