I just wanted to tell everyone thanks for all the advice.

I decided to stop reading and start doing and just see how a day would
do without using the word "no" as was being discussed.

We had fettucine alfredo for breakfast and are now eating snickers and
carrots for lunch (nice combo, eh?)lol

Both my girls are eating in their underwear.


Just a week ago I would have said "no, we're having this for
breakfast" or "its time to get dressed". Thanks for helping me think
outside the box.

Everyone is happy today (so far) with no tantrums.

And despite the snickers and carrots, lol, so far- no casualties :)
