I think some unschoolers use umbrella schools like Alternative Ed
Institute (they have a website) where you pay a small fee and send
in attendance records (an email showing dates) and that's it. Once
you are registered with the umbrella, you don't have to do any of
the other stuff directly with the school district/state.
