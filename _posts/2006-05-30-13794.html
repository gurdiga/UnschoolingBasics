What an awesome experience it was for me to hear my dh Larry at our discussion group 'Fears and Self Doubt' The topic was one i picked specifically to help me in dealing with his fears and doubts (to give him an opportunity to hear other's beside myself respond to his concerns)...and then when he agreed to co-facilitate the group -well that was exciting, but the real thrill was to hear him speak and lead the group. He was the person i fell in love with 25 years ago (way before kids). Exactly why we came to the first conference two years ago - hoping that what he has in his heart would resurface.

Being around so many thoughtful folks is soooo important. As an unschooling family, going to conferences are as important as putting gas in our car.
