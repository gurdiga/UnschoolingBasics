For more information, please also consider joining one or both of the following yahoo
groups:

1) Terlingua Ranch

This is a place for all that are interested in The Ranch, the Big Bend Area, Terlingua, Study
Butte, and any other areas in the Trans-Pecos. This is the place to exchange information,
ideas and pleasant conversations whether you are a property owner or just interested in
the area. Everyone is welcome to post links to interesting websites, photos, or even
recipes. Check out the link section where you will find some helpful information.

2) Texas Outback


A Texas Outback board (formerly CheapCamping cheap camping ),for those who crisscross
the Greater Big Bend
seeking adventure! Looking for the best Mexican food, bookstores,
campsites, scenic views, or watering holes? Learn where the best
place to buy prescription meds or dental work in Mexico. For those who
come for a week or a lifetime. Dedicated to those who love America's
best kept secret, the Texas Outback. Let's talk Terlingua, Study Butte, Lajitas, Marathon,
Marfa, Van Horn, Alpine, Fort Stockton, Big Bend National Park, hot springs, Big Bend State
Park, Presidio, Ft. Davis, Pecos, Sanderson, Del Rio and all points near and in between.

Let's discuss tents, vehicle living, cheap shelters and other camping, traveling and living
interests and let's talk about the ins and outs of buying West Texas land.
