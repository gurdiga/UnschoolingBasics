Carolina Homeschooler

6 Jul 2009, at 8:10pm

Join Carolina Homeschooler in October or November as we take
our fourth and fifth trips to Washington DC. (We had so much
interest last year, we're organizing two trips again this year!)

We're planning to tour the various memorials and monuments,
White House, Old Post Office Pavilion and Tower, Arlington
Cemetery, US Capitol, Library of Congress, Bureau of Engraving
and Printing, the Holocaust Museum, the Pentagon, Ford's Theater,
and Union Station. We'll also have the opportunity to visit
Smithsonian museums such as: Air and Space Museum, Museum of
Natural History, Museum of American History (recently reopened
after renovations), Museum of the American Indian, and others of
your choice.

Learn more about our October trip here:
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelwdc.htm

Learn more about our November trip here:
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelwdc2.htm

These trips filled quickly last year, so please register as soon as
you're certain you'd like to go. We'll keep a waiting list once we've
reached our maximum. The registration deadline for this trip is
August 15.

To get periodic updates about all of our trips, subscribe to our
Traveling Homeschoolers mailing list at
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/lists.htm

Happy Traveling,
Dianna
