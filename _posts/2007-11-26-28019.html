Greetings from the 2008 Unschooling Conference in Madison, Wisconsin.

Our 2007 conference, the first unschooling conference ever in Wisconsin, was
a huge success! It was a very energizing and renewing experience.
Participants took full advantage of the small size of the conference and the
intimate atmosphere to connect and have in-depth conversations with other
conference attendees and presenters. Feedback was very enthusiastic, and
many people asked for another conference in 2008, so here we go.

The 2008 Unschooling Conference, January 12, will feature a keynote address
by Manish Jain of Shikshantar: The Peoples' Institute for Rethinking
Development and Education, in Udaipur, India. We are looking forward to
another exciting and fulfilling day of connecting with unschoolers, and
expanding our unschooling horizons.

For more information and to download a registration form, please visit our
website: www.unschoolingconference.com. We look forward to seeing everyone
in January!

Tanya Cunningham
The Unschooling Conference
www.unschoolingconference.com
