Okay, I have to share something remarkable with you all. (You all -- not
y'all -- this is a NORTHEAST conference.) I had a really, really, really good
time this weekend!

Conference planning and running is exhausting, and this one certainly was.
And it can be stressfull, and there were moments of that too. But I have to say
the atmosphere and energy at this conference was amazing and spectacular and
sweet and loving and fun and supportive.

From new unschoolers to oldtimers, from gorgeous wee babes to those hugging,
funny teens to caring and working and playing moms and dads, you were open
to learning new things, to laughing and finding the joy, and creating
community.

Some moments stand out for me: The glowing, lovely Jillian, who charmed all
of us, along with her very cool mama Angel; Our Famous Ren, who along with
her two talks and Funshop, also made many of us up for hours, creating an
interesting new look for Ben Lovejoy and butterflies for young admirers; the
unrelenting, albeit odd, loving support of Jon Kream, and the spectacular arts and
crafts room from Rue; the hugs and knowing of Ben and Kelly; the way 25-30
people happily worked together to clean the arts and crafts room in less time
than we could have imagined; watching Julian spend blissful time with loving
looks with his first love; the really neat conference 'zine, the DOZENS of
people who told me and Beth that this has changed their lives.... Wow...

You are all amazing and I love you all.
Kathryn
