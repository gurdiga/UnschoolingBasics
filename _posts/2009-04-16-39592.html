What are you supposed to do with test results? That would be my big
question.

Some states don't do anything with them, they don't even want to see
them, you're just required to test.

Other states will want to see that the child tests in the top 50% of
age related children. If they don't, then what? Can you test again?

I would honestly look more into that before I worry.
