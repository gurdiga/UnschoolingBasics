Education is Obsolete (May 26, 2008)
By: Rabbi David Lapin


Education is Obsolete shiur is on a rather radical model for
elementary and high-school education going forward. Never before has
Torah been positioned as the most relevant educational force not only
for religion and personal development, but also for a new generation
of digital thinkers: the under 25-year olds who I have called (using
my own under-25-year-old son's term) E-Geners, or the generation of
exponential thinkers. I explain how nothing caters to the needs of
this generation better than an outstanding Torah education topped up
with world class Math and English. Armed with these skills, young
people could quickly qualify for any university entrance they please,
grow as Torah thought leaders, or become entrepreneurs in their own
rights.
