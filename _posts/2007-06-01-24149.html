Hi Nicole,

My three children had been in foster care for four years before we
met and adopted them. That temporary kind of lifestyle is very hard
on children and a lot develop some sort of attachment issues. Do a
search on it and see if anything applies. You (or her parents) can
take a look at my blog and click the "adoption" label in the sidebar
for more information or e-mail me offlist.

The best you can do is listen to her. Do they know if she was abused
in any way while in state care? Also, make sure the parents get
copies of the reports from the hospital.

How well did you know this child before this happened? Did you have
a trusting relationship?

I'll keep her in my thoughts. Please let me know if I can help.

~ Joanne ~
