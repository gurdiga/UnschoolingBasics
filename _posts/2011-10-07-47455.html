As wonderful as it is to have a whole bunch of unschoolers in one "place" please refrain from posting "anyone in my area?" messages and/or replying to them on-list if they do come through, Especially Americans! There's a whole long page of American email lists and groups in the link below and if you know of a list or group that isn't there, let me know and I'll pass the info on to Sandra (or send it directly to Sandra if you'd rather):

http://sandradodd.com/world#us

If you scroll up that page there are more links to other regional groups as well as special-interest groups (religious based and other):
http://sandradodd.com/world

Once again, if you know of a list or group that isn't there, pass on the info! If you live in an area where even homeschooling info is thin on the ground, Sandra also includes more general home-ed sites.

Thanks y'all!
