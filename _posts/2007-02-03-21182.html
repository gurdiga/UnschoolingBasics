3 Feb 2007, at 12:23pm

Issue 4 of *Connections: ezine of unschooling and mindful parenting* is
now available online: http://connections.organiclearning.org

I'm excited to announce that Robyn Coburn has joined our team of regular
contributing writers, and she has a great article in this month's issue
titled, "She's Not Me," celebrating all the wonderful differences
between mother and daughter.

Of course, Issue 4 features other great regular columns like "Ask Rue,"
with Rue Kream, author of _Parenting a Free Child_, "Oh, How We Shine!"
with Anne Ohman and "Unschooling Dads" with Ben Lovejoy along with
articles by Sandra Dodd and Deb Lewis. This month our feature articles
by Kathryn Baptista and Pam Laricchia delve into regional conference
planning, offering hints and tips on pulling together a successful
unschooling conference, and our "Inspiration Station" offers a
fascinating article on the Magic of Tarot by Amy Carpenter-Leugs. Hot
Topics from several email lists cover topics like unschooling faith,
socialization, and when parents say "no."

Enjoy, and thank you for your support!
