Joyce Fetteroll

10 Jun 2010, at 11:05am

Write an alphabet where each alphabetic monster is bent on alphabetic
mayhem on the next alphabetic beast. Where the Tentacled Terror tenses
to tear into the Ulcerated Underdweller who upchucks upon the Venomous
Villain as he voraciously vilifies ...


Idea from The Absolutely Awful Alphabet by Mordicai Gerstein.


Joyce
Alphabet of horrors
