There's one in April: LIFE is Good in Corvallis, Oregon, hosted by Mary
Gold.

I know there's one in Wisconsin, but I'm sorry I don't have details.

Cindy Fox and the Arizona unschoolers are hosting a homeschooling
conference that may have an unschooling *feel* to it (later this month,
I think).

Pam Laricchia's Toronto Unschooling Conference in May, I believe.

California hosts the HSC---not strictly unschooling, but many
unschoolers go---in August.

Barb Lundgren's Rethinking Education in Texas the Labor Day weekend
before the L&L.

You could also google "unschooling conference" and see what hits you
get.

This is a FAR cry from what was offered just five years ago!!!

You could also plan your own! <bwg> If you plan it, they will come!
