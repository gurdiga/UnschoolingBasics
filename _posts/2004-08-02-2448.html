The last two years have been in Columbia, SC. This year it is hosted by
Kathryn Baptista and is located in Peabody, MA. August 27-29.

It's a wonderful weekend full of unschoolers----over 400 of us! <g> It's
non-age-discriminatory---everyone can participate in any event (presentation or
funshop). There'll be a talent show, a RAFFLE!!!!!!, and an optional whale
watch.

Go to the site: _www.liveandlearnconference.org_
(http://www.liveandlearnconference.org) for more info. You WON'T be disappointed!

Kathryn will be taping the presentations this year. She'll let you know when
they are available.

Tapes of the last two years are avaliable now:
_www.schoolsoutsupport.org/resources_ (http://www.schoolsoutsupport.org/resources)

They will also be available at this year's conference. I'll have a table.
Stop by and see me!
