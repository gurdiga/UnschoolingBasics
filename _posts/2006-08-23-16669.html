I've been reading a great book that has changed my whole outlook on
life. I took it out at the library but I'm going to have to buy it.
It's called Choosing Happiness by Alexandra Stoddard. If you've never
read it, please do. It has helped me to be more joyful, and to enjoy
each moment as it comes. I am definitely one of those who is guilty
of "thinking" about all of the things I "have to" do, instead of
enjoying what I'm doing at that present moment. Again, Choosing
Happiness by Alexandra Stoddard.
