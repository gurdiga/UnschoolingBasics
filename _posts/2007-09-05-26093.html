Hi,

I have had numerous offlist conversations with people like myself who have 1 child in a school while unschooling other children at home. I have created a yahoo groups site to talk about the unique challenges our families face as we live an unschool lifestyle with school right in our homes. This is also a good place to get support if your unschooled child is curious about school.

Web address is http://groups.yahoo.com/group/unschoolingwithschool

This is the very first group I have ever started and I am newer than new as a moderator, so any wise help offers are welcome.

Mary Hickman
unschooling 2 children 10 and 8 while my almost 13 year old attends public school her choice.
