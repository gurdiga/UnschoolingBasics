REGISTRATION IS NOW OPEN!!!














Enjoy Life Unschooling

Conference

21-22 September 2009
in Frederick, MD
at the beautiful Mar Lu Conference Center

Speakers include:

Danielle Conger









Kelly Lovejoy
Elissa Cleaveland
Heather Burditt
Zoa Connor









and
TJ Phillips leading the SSUDs

Funshops galore!
Hula hopping and hula hoop making
Button making
Video games

henna

black velvet art
