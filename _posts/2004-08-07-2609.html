and spent an hour looking at them on the web. Now I know about them and I

YES!!
Now you're onto something a few of us at this list are passionate about...YURTS.:)
I love the idea of living in a yurt style house actually. There's an unschooler out in AZ living in a round house (inspired by yurts) and I believe Kelly and Ben's next house will be a round house.

If you think yurts are cool, check into cob houses! Oh my gosh, they are awesome. I want to build one in my backyard so bad....
It's an earth house (actually, look up earth houses at google, there are all kinds) that is SCULPTED. Can you imagine a better project for an artistic soul? Sculpting your home? I want a cob house!!

Ren

OK. so I ask my dh about cob houses, cause I just think he knows anything

look. so cool!
