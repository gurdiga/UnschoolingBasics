Below is from Sandra Dodd. She is not on UnschoolingBasics, soI
frequently send her posts that I think are inspiring and that I think
could make a difference to someone else's unschooling life.

They are enshrined forever at the glorious resource that is Sandra's
website. If you've never been, you need to make a trip there. And if
you *have* been by before, you really need to go again: she adds
something almost every day.

It's a great place to send those who question unschooling: they can
read for days and weeks and months and still not cover the whole thing!

Also not to miss: Joyce Fetteroll's site: www.JoyfullyRejoycing.com

So, if you have a story about your unschooling teen, you may send it on
to Sandra directly, or just post it here, and I'll make sure it gets
moved there.
