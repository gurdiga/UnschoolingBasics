The Home Education Network of Arizona is an inclusive statewide
support organization that respects, values, and finds strength
in diversity. We are neutral in matters of religion and partisan
politics and we respect each other's educational choices. HENA's
mission is to create an environment in which all home educating
families in Arizona can share resources, find support, and build
community.
