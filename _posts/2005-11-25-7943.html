I don't know. I hate shopping. I'm disabled and use a scooter in stores and
you don't know how many rude, inconsiderate jerks there are. We HAD to go
today to get some things (we're getting a puppy tomorrow, needed some stuff!),
and the kids needed boots. I had a woman ram me with her cart, a few others cut
me off, my husband was getting very angry. We also had the 3 kids in tow.
One guy almost rammed our van when we were parking in the handicapped parking.
