***...I'm still learning about some of the "unparenting" philosophies,...***

This is not an unparenting philosophy list. For one thing, unparenting is a word some people have used against radical unschoolers as an accusation of neglect. An insult. But as a philosophy unparenting has more to do with law of attraction ideas and libertarian politics than it does unschooling, (http://www.unparenting.com/ ) though many people who follow the unparenting philosophy are no fans of public education. It's not the same as unschooling and it's not what we talk about here.
