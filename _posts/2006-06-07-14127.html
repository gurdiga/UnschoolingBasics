If you can't make it to Albuquerque, for the Live and Learn
conference, there is a very unschooling-friendly conference in
August, in Sacramento, sponsored by HSC. It includes a very large
teen program and most of the teens who participate are unschoolers or
very relaxed homeschoolers. Many from your area.

Look at <www.hsc.org> and click on the 2006 conference link for more
information.
