liveandlearnconference.org - technically it's over the river in
Illinois (Fairview Heights - still pretty close to you, we're driving
out from CT. Where in St Louis are you? I lived in Rock Hill way back
in the late 80s) Great time - no one would pressure him to do
anything, there's usually a Dad's gathering (SSUDS - secret society of
unschooling dads) organized by a convert to unschooling with
the 'street cred' to impress any suspicious dad - former military, by
the book type guy who is now sold on unschooling and living by
principle rather than rules. But the best part of the conference is
seeing the living evidence - lots of KIDS of all ages just being and
doing their thing. BTW the fuzzy guy coordinating the Network Gaming
funshop is my DH (note there are NO *work*shops).

--Deb

Hi, Cinder. I found this e-mail in my in box and am just now getting
around to answering a few posts.

We're saying that the conference is in St Louis, 'though technically it
is in Fairview Heights, IL---just across the river on I-64. The website
is www.liveandlearnconference.org We also have an e-list for updates
and conference chat at liveandlearnconference@yahoogroups.com

There will be many dads there, as this is a family affair. Lots of
unschooled kids to meet and watch first hand. There will be
presentations by long-time unschooling parents, along with a few grown
unschoolers---ready to answer your questions! There's a group just for
dads: SUDDs (Secret Society of Unschooling Dads).

It's a really fun weekend for all ages!

~Kelly
