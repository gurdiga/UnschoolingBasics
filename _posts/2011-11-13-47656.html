If you can afford it you should get an Xbox 360 or PS3. PS3 includes online
play for free but Xbox requires an xbox live membership. That is where the
good games are for older kids.

Wii is great for young kids and sports and the interactive games like Just
Dance. (We LOVE Just Dance) But our boys always head for the other machines
for shooter games. Resident Evil (our kids play Resident Evil 5) is an all
time favorite though and I see two of them are on Wii.

Back to your question though, start with Resident Evil 4. I have never seen
that second one, Resident Evil Archives.
