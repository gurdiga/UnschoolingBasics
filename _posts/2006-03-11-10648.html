Raw milk is only legal in 28 of the 50 states. It's a damn shame too.
I remember buying raw milk (both goat and cow) at the local health
food store in Portland many years ago.
Then came the huge warning labels, then it disappeared entirely.

In Pensacola, right before we moved, the local health food co-op
started selling raw milk labeled "for animal consumption only". So
folks are finding ways around the laws.

Here's some info. on it, and links to places where you can find raw milk:
http://www.realmilk.com/happening.html

I think these kinds of issues are WAY beyond what government should
control. It's my own choice to drink raw milk or not, what business
does the government have in that? Another law I will ignore at will.
