22 Aug 2009, at 2:04am

Join Carolina Homeschooler this October as we take our fourth
trip to Washington DC. We're planning to tour the White House,
Arlington Cemetery, US Capitol, Library of Congress, Bureau of
Engraving and Printing, the Holocaust Museum, Ford's Theater,
various memorials and monuments, and more. We'll also have the
opportunity to visit Smithsonian museums such as: Air and Space
Museum, Museum of Natural History, Museum of American History,
Museum of the American Indian, and others of your choice. Space
is limited, and our registration deadline is August 25, with final
balances due on August 31.

Learn more about our October trip here:
http://www.carolinahomeschooler.com/travelwdc.htm

To get periodic updates about all of our trips, subscribe to our
