Yeah, a question I feel qualified to answer!

Blogging is the verb of creating a "web log", which has morphed into
"blog". It can be like an online diary or more like a bunch of links with
commentary, or a combination of the two. There are many free blog hosting
sites. I use http://blogger.com .
