http://www.homeschooling-wpa.org

This is the Wisconsin Parents Association, a fabulous organization
that in the 80s got the *very* lenient Wisconsin homeschooling law
passed and continues to be a strong grassroots organization that (very
successfully) defends homeschooling freedom in Wisconsin. It's run by
Larry and Susan Kaseman who have a column in Home Education Magazine.
They know the law inside and out, have written a great book about
homeschooling in Wisconsin (available for sale on the Website), send
out a quarterly newsletter with updates, and organize a good
conference every spring with lots of unschooling-friendly workshops.
They unschooled four (now grown) kids themselves. WPA should be the
first and main resource for every Wisconsin home/unschooler.
