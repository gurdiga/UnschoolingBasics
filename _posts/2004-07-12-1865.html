"I'm contemplating coming to the conference, and wanted to know if,

Well....I've never gone alone, but I've never had dh there either.
wink, wink.

weekend makes it MORE than worth it! Truly.
The conferences were the highlight trips of my kids lives....and
that's competing with their cousins in AK.
