Info I got from another group. See below:

Vanessa:

My sister lives in France and my understanding is that Homeschool laws are
similar to what we have in FL-- you register your intent to Homeschool and
then have yearly evaluations or tests. This organization is the big
homeschool group, she should contact them with any questions:
http://www.lesenfantsdabord.org/index.php .

As for vaccinations--I'm not sure what the laws are, but I do know that
France's healthcare system allows for alternative health providers. My
sister was seeing a homeopath who advised her to stop vaccinating her kids.
She ended up still vaccinating but selectively and on an extended schedule.
But if the homeopath was advising it, I have to assume there's a legal way
to avoid vax.

The next time I speak to my sis, I will ask for more info on that!
