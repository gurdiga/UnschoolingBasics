Check out the first annual Northeast Unschooling Conference website
for information on the wonderful event you will be supporting.
www.northeastunschoolingconference.com
The Scholarship Raffle is run like an old fashioned "penny social,"
where entrants place their tickets in cups corresponding to the items
they would like to win. Proceeds fund registrations for attendees of
limited financial means.

If you have an interesting, fun or unique item to donate for this
event, please contact us.

Some ideas :

New OR Used books, games, toys;

handmade items are especially popular:
jewelry, clothing, small quilts, artwork, collectibles

CDs, DVD's, video games, antique or vintage items, posters, outdoor
gear; magazine subscriptions, craft and art supplies or kits, musical
instruments

homegrown vegetables, canned or jarred goods, (we've seen a box of
potatoes and a jar of backyard honey in past years!)

health & beauty aids (herbal lotions, homemade soaps; please include a
list of ingredients)

gift certificates for business services

Unschooling means learning from living so let your imagination run wild!
