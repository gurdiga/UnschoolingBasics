Hello everyone! I am very excited to announce several new things.
NETSHU, http://groups.yahoo.com/group/NETSHU/ , is now open to
children of all ages. It started out mainly for children 10-16 but we
have various ages now due to younger siblings and some families with
young children only. We still hope to continue getting some older
children (above 10) in the group as they seem to be the minority now
a days. But as I said we are now welcoming all ages!

Now for the exciting part! We now have a facility for all of our
activities and such. We also have a Gym class lined
up......Yayyyyyyy! I know a lot of you have been hoping for such a
class to help the children get the exercise they need. I will be
going Saturday morning to finalize everything on use of the building.
Starting in April we will be having some sort of activity going on
every Friday from 12:00 - 2:00. I am open to ideas and volunteers of
all kind. I hope to do a cooking class around once a month, some type
of arts & craft class once a month, Gym class once a month maybe
twice depending on what everyone else would like to do.

I have a million ideas running through my head and would love to hear
from those of you interested in joining our Fun Fridays! We welcome
those from any group, you do not have to belong to NETSHU, but it
would be nice :) Just keep in mind we are a secular group.

Please start either posting, emailing or calling me with what you
think. Wether you want to join in the fun, have ideas, want to
volunteer or anything. We are excited to finally have more activities
for our children to join in on!
