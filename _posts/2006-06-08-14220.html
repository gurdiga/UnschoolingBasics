Disney has a wonderful college program and intern program for
preforming arts. They get a chance to get some "professional
time"under their wings and beef up their resume. There used to be a
young girl at my church who was so gung-ho on being a Broadway
actress. She went to college and found out about this program. Every
summer she would go there and work and she did a 2 year internship
there after she graduated from college. She loved it! I wish I knew
what she was doing now because she was one of those really neat kids
that was truly living her passion. I don't know if she ever made it
to Broadway, but I do know that she had many wonderful adventures
getting there!
