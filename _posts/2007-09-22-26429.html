Greetings all!

I wanted to let folks know about a new statewide Maryland unschooling
list called Maryland Organic Learning:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/marylandorganiclearning/

Our goal is to create a statewide forum for Maryland unschoolers to
connect and network. We hope to create a space to plan future outings
and workshops, to mobilize on legal issues, and to support each other
and our children. We hope you'll join us!
