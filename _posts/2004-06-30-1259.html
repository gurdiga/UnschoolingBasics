Chicago Unschoolers Workshop

A day-long workshop for unschooling families and those interested in finding
out more about this unique approach to education. The Workshop is held every
other year. The 2004 Workshop Speakers were Sandra Dodd, Dorothy Werner, Alison
McKee and Jean Vondriska.

The next workshop is scheduled for Saturday, September 9, 2006, 9:00am to
5:30pm. It will be held at the Portage Park Center for the Arts, 5801 W. Dakin in
Chicago.
