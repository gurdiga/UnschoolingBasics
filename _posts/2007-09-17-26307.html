I have one on the top of my foot, but it's my only one (for now anyway....)
so I can't compare pain. It's small so it was quick. I always say it felt
like a hot cat scratch. The color fades faster on your feet. Some tattoo
technicians won't tattoo you on your foot or won't guarantee the work. Mine
did fade a bit, but I'm still happy with it 10 years later. My sister got
one on the bottom of her foot and it's almost completely faded.
HTH's!
