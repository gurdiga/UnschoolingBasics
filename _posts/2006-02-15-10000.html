"Please tell me more about ATC"

ATC's are Art Trading Cards. They are little works of art 2.5"x3.5",
with the artist's information on back. They can not be sold, only
traded with other ATC folks.:)

Here's some detailed info: http://www.cedarseed.com/air/atc.html

And if you decide to join Imagination Tribe, we have photos of our
recent ATC trade as well as the art/craft projects of the various
members that choose to post photos.

There are many of us that have found such a support group very
helpful for overcoming fears of inadequacy in regards to art/writing
etc.... It IS a support group rather than a discussion group. It
exists to help people nourish their creative selves, so it's a very
positive place.

Ren
learninginfreedom.com

Imagination Tribe!!!:)

And maybe the list will inspire you to let go of your old school
hurts....that's really why I created it in the first place. Healing
can be assisted when you have support, and realize that most of us are
dealing with (or have dealt with) many of the same fears.

I'm going to post an excerpt here from the Imagination Circle Journal
that the group created last year. My friend Amy in Pensacola wrote
this on one of her pages:

"I almost passed this journal along without contributing. Something
about this makes me feel naked. Like the dream I had in school where
I go onto the bus having forgottten my clothes.
I think this feeling of insecurity and impotence---I know I have
something to say but lack the courage or ability to say it---that
feeling comes from my days in a classroom. Then, independent thought
wasn't ok. Well to hell with that! It's time to reclaim my voice."

What does this have to do with unschooling? Everything!!
I think our ability to move confidently in the direction of our dreams
(as Thoreau once said) and interests, hugely influences how effective
we are in assisting our children in creating the live of their
choosing. It's time to heal!! It's time to pull out the crayons and
paint and PLAY...just because it feels good. No more judging yourself
as inferior, no more comparisons.
