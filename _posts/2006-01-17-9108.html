The Educational Freedom
Index<http://www.manhattan-institute.org/html/cr_14.htm>is out! How
free is YOUR state?

This little report is more than simply a listing of which states keep out of
your business with regard to the school choices you make for your family -
it also shows how freedom and success correlate - in
this<http://www.manhattan-institute.org/html/cr_14.htm#Does%20Education%20Freedom%20Help>section
the authors find that it correlates with better NAEP and SAT scores
- moreso than wealth, class size, spending per student and percent of
minority students COMBINED.

It also has a ranking of freedom with regard to Availability of
Homeschooling Options<http://www.manhattan-institute.org/html/cr_14_tables_7_and_8.htm>.
I was disappointed in Illinois' ranking - maybe we can get this turned
around somehow though...
