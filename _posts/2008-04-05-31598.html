asked him where they came from and he said pokemon diamond! Apparently
I looked confused, because he continued to inform me that you visit

new saying?" You say yes or no, and if you say yes he gives you a new
word!

density,flambe,neutrino,cubism,GMT,polyphenol and starboard.

Just something I can keep in mind when I start getting worried that
video games are not time well spent!
