What sorts of things do your kids want to do in the city? Can you plan day trips and even overnights? Are there any groups of homeschoolers you can connect with so you have people to visit?

It's pretty extreme to uproot people from their community and established friendships - and cities aren't necessarily welcoming places. If your kids have a comfortable social group to move To, then they might feel better about visiting older friends in the country on weekends.
