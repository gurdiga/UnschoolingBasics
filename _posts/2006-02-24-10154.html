24 Feb 2006, at 9:56am

My heart broke when I read Ren's post about Hannah. It made me think back
to the posts about parents feeling resentful about cleaning up behind our
children or being overwhelmed with clothes to wash and not feeling like we
have any help from our children. Today I will feel joy over messes to clean
up behind and clothes to wash and fold. Because today I have the privilege
of being home with my girls and enjoy life with them. Today I can enjoy
their laughs and fusses! Wow, how one event like this can bring gratitude
for what one has to a whole new level. My thoughts and prayers of comfort
are surrounding this family today.

~Tracey in S.C. - mom to Rachael (13) & Madison (10)
