I am just posting to get it off my chest ...and make an invitation!
After we got back from that magical week at L & L in September I have
been really feeling lonely.
There is no one around here I can have an usnchooling conversaion
with or juat talk about kids. There is one more unschooler in town
but even her two older dds are in school right now due to her health
and other family reasons.
The other one I visit once a month lives about 1:40 minutes away and
we can talk and have some good conversations but I need more.
My kids need more. Our little homeschooling group is nice but after L
& L it just won't do. Too many talks about curriculum, disabilities,
etc
Moving is out of the question since our bussiness is our farm and we
need to be around here.
I would travel more but everyone is so far away and my little dd just
hates car drives. Kelli Traaseth, you are like 5 hours away right?????
Soooooooooooooooo.......
I already invited people at AU to come over and visit our Dairy Farm
anytime.
I want to invite any of you that would like to come spend a few days
at our Dairy Farm in SE MN.\
My dd will be 2 in January and ds is 5 and loves to play video games
( specially Super Mario). We have a Wii,a DS and he would love other
kids to play with. He gets along with kids of any age.
Any takers????????

Lonely out in the country,
Alex
