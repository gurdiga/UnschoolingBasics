I live in Richardson, about 30 minutes away. Texas is VERY friendly towards
homeschooling in general, and my wife and I are members of at least two
(that I know of) local unschooling groups. The annual Rethinking Education
conference is held in Irving, or thereabouts, which is about 20 minutes away
from Lewisville. There's a bunch of us here.

As I understand homeschooling laws here in Texas, it actually rests on a
couple of court decisions. I don't believe we even have annual or regular
academic "equivalency" tests (y'all with older kids, correct me on this if
I'm wrong).
