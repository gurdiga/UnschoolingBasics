they collectively paid $9.33. $28/3.~~

No, they each paid $9 (or $27 total). The problem is that you need to
subtract the $2 the bellhop kept, not add it. The hotel actually
collected $25, not $30. The problem is set up to trick you into adding
the $2, rather than subtracting it which would make it correct.
