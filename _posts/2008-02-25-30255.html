25 Feb 2008, at 9:54pm

Dear Folks,

I am so sorry to tell you that Lisa Heyman crossed over today at
12:30 p.m. with her husband, Larry, at her side. Larry and Roxanne
and Ruby are surrounded by the amazing, loving community that has
grown up around them, and they are feeling great love and support.

If you are in the area, you are invited to attend a service for Lisa
this Wednesday, February 27, at 11:00. It will be held at the King
David Cemetery, 101 Mill Street, Putnam Valley, New York. Here is a
link to mapquest for directions:

http://tinyurl.com/3bb3et

Also, if you are in the area and would like to pay a condolence call,
they will be receiving visitors at their home Wednesday and
Thursday. The address there is 12 Mathes Street, Lake Peekskill,
NY. (Please note that parking can be tricky on their street.)

If you would like to send anything to the girls, please use this address:

Larry, Roxanne & Ruby Gauchman
P.O. Box 430
Lake Peekskill, NY 10537

Finally, if you do plan to come to the memorial service for Lisa,
there are two things you should know. First, they have requested
that folks refrain from wearing all black in deference to and in
celebration of Lisa's amazing brilliance. Second, Ruby (a/k/a Fire)
requested that people honor their children's needs by not bringing
them to the service if they do not wish to be there.

Please feel free to share this message with anyone you feel would want to know.


In deep gratitude,

Marji
