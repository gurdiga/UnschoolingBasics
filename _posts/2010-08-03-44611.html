You'll want to check local unschooling/home ed lists for particulars about laws and paperwork.

Since I don't know what you know in general, here are some good, "starter" resources:

http://sandradodd.com/beginning
http://sandradodd.com/pam/principles

http://joyfullyrejoycing.com/

A good source of unschooling blogs and articles:
http://enjoylifeunschooling.com/

My favorite blog, with a nicely organized collection of posts on unschooling:
http://sites.google.com/site/dragonflykaizen/unschooling-1

A good way to "start" though, is to live like you are on vacation - not a dull vacation where you're grateful for school to start up again, but the best kind of vacation, where you go places and do things out of curiositiy or enthusiasm - or snuggle up together enjoying one anothers' company.
