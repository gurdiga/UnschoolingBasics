it has come
an official unschooling guild has come into existence
the name is HORDE OF UNSCHOOLERS
the server is
THE VENTURE CO
it is a horde guild

I know it is hard to begin a new character but think of the fun,
there will be guild activities ( you can see some of the goofy ideas
at the worlrdofwarcraft.com site for guild activities.
this is a once in my lifetime oppurtunity.
I know it is hard to switch servers there were 17 different servers
of people who replied we chose one that no one had ( other than a
high level horde who said they would help but couldn't leave their
guild
enjoy the pain
we don't have the guild web site up yet but have a charter and are
now beginning
we hope to get team speak so we can talk in the game but I haven't
figured out the way to arrange it ( anyone know?)
we can use skype in the game
if you down load skype.com you can call and talk to anyone online
who has skype , even as the game is running ; I know you can have
multiple people online but not sure if it will work for whole guild.
we are in orgimar look for Ierezak, chronica and zzina ( we will
change to blood elves in the expansion, though.
please show solidarity
patrick andrea and adli
