Update on Jay and Betsy:

I've given them some of the donations you all sent...they were

some cash (from all the donations) ready for them and had planned to
take it over the next day.
Jay had shown up at our door at about 10pm on Thursday night,

boost that night.
He was almost in tears.

They are more grateful for all the concern and want you all to know

forget all the kindness they've encountered from unschoolers they

Katie is coming over soon to take home a big bagful of all the art
supplies/clothing items that have also been sent.

Thank you again everyone.
