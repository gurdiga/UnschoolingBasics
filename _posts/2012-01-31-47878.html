Hello All, This is just a short blurb to intro us to the group. My name is sarah and we are an Christian Unschooling Family living in Ontario Canada. We have homeschooled/Unschooled now for 14 years and I feel like I am jsut getting my feet wet with all the cool things that life learning has to offer. I have been on groups before years back, but slowly fell away from reading them and then my children decided to try out school in September. They hated it , and 4/5 are now back home as of 4 weeks ago. GLEE. Our oldest who was unschooled his entire life decided to go to highschool this year and loves it. In fact he holds a 90 some odd percent average and even has been excempted from exams this week, so yes unschooling works lol
We feel truly blessed to be able to live the dream that we live and I look forward to exploring, talking and getting to know people on this list!
Have a great day!
Sarah
