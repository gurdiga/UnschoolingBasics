Hello,

I am not new to this group, mostly just taking things in...

I have recently started a blog carnival with the intention of uniting
some of us who blog about our unschooling experiences. As far as I
know, Unschooling Voices (a previous unschooling blog carnival) is no
longer being published. Here is the link to the carnival I have
created http://blogcarnival.com/bc/cprof_2356.html

If you are unfamiliar with blog carnivals (I was not too long ago!),
they are blog posts published on a regular shedule (in this case
monthly) with links to others' blog posts on a related theme/topic.

The first edition is due to be published on my blog September 10.

Hope to hear from some of you :)
