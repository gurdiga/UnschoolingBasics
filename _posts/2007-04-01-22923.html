I'm not sure if this is legal, but she ought to consider it good advertisement since I'm including the source: Fresh from the Vegetarian Slow Cooker by Robin Robertson.

5c oats
1c sliced almonds
3/4c craisins
1/2c raisins
1/2c sesame seeds
2/3c maple syrup
1/4c canola
1/4t cinnamon

Mix all ingredients in crockpot. Do *not* cover with lid. Cook for 1.5 hours on high, then turn to low and cook an additional 2 hours. Stir occasionally. Spread on cookie sheets to cool and dry to crispy. Yields about 8c.

Obviously you can experiment with the ingredients. I really like the craisins though. I haven't tried reducing the syrup or canola because I'm not sure how much the liquid is necessary for the cooking process.

Enjoy,
Katharine
