25 Jul 2004, at 12:16pm

"So I guess I just need some support that having such a passive day is ok.
she doesn't seem unhappy, we have not had a fight, she has eaten well and I
think she might have learned some things. If she was bored we would have had
less contentment and more agitation I think."

Oh my....you're 6months pregnant and feeling guilty for wanting a break!? Get all those "shoulds" your mother put into your head OUT of there!:)
If your child is happy, if you're enjoying yourself, there is no problem. I think it's really good for us to know our limits, to care for ourselves with "down" days. What kind of example is it to our children if we never know our limits or say "I'm too tired to do anything right now"?
If you were exhausted and your child needed something more, it's time for your dh to take over! Or someone else.....
We certainly avoid the outdoors right now, unless we're swimming or at the beach. I think you and your family should do what works for YOU all, and forget about what your mother thinks.
Sounds like a happy, pleasant day to me.

Ren
