"This is amazing!"

Yes and no.

I am still likely to say "WOW" internally sometimes...because natural
learning is something I did not get to understand and observe until
*I* fully let go. But if you think about it, it isn't amazing in an
unusual way....it's just how humans learn. We're so schooled that it
will probably amaze us for the rest of our lives though.:)

On the other hand, humans ARE amazing, interesting creatures...so
maybe how they learn is just amazing regardless of whether you were
schooled or not.

Sometimes, when I point out some learning moment that was really
cool, Trevor (15) just looks at me and says "well yeah Mom", as if it
wasn't amazing at all. Their world is so very different from our own.
Isn't it cool!?
