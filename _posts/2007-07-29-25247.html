When i was 18 i gave a baby up for adoption, his mom BFed him for nearly 20
months. It took her 6 months to get her milk in, it was easier for her then
for some because she had, had a baby when she was 19 so she had previously
lactated. She used a drug (i dont remember the exact name i know it was a nose
spray) that the side effect was lactation, then she pumped and pumped and
pumped for 6 months till she finally started producing enough to actually BF.
It isnt an easy process and it is not 100% that she will lactate but it's
worth a try.
