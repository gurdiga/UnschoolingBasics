I hope you don't mind if I brag a bit... I'm still in amazement myself.

Normally, if I start yakking about something my DH isn't 100% interested in,
he sort of tunes me out. Now, where the kids are concerned, the "technical"
stuff sometimes is over his head. So, sometimes I assume he's not paying
attention. lol... boy, have I been wrong! Now, I know he's had his qualms over
the homeschooling issue, the same typical concerns of the uninformed. And, of
course, I've always had an answer/solution/suggestion for those concerns.

Now, I've been VERY VERY lucky in that, I have full support from his mom and
my parents as well as some extended family members. The grandmothers have
even expressed interest in being involved. So, we were on a mini-vacation with
my mother-in-law this past weekend and the topic of homeschooling came up.
Imagine my surprise and AWE when DH basically turned into the BIGGEST
unschooling advocate! He does get it! LOL I had printed some things out and stuck them
on the bathroom reading pile, and he has been reading it, I guess. I'm just
super happy that he's totally on the same page as me when I was thinking we
were in two different books! LOL

Oh, and my mother-in-law is also on the same page (and she was a teacher
once!).
