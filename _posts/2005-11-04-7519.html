I keep coming back to this thought.....know your child!

they can't figure out what to eat because nothing sounds good and they are tired
of the same things.

couple of years ago, but we moved away from the chiropractor that sold them to
us. The other day I was thinking that the food issue was not as big a deal
when we lived in our previous home and I remembered the enzymes!
