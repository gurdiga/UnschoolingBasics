I am posting this for any of you with an interest in taking a survey.

It's certainly not about unschooling---just check out the questions. <g>

But if you'd like to contribute to her study...


********************************


I am a home schooling parent completing a graduate degree in School
Psychology from the Citadel. I plan to work with home-schooling
families. My
research thesis is on reasons families choose to home-school and the
needs/concerns they have. I will be sharing the results of my research
with
teachers and school psychologists, helping them to better understand the
home schooling population.

I am requesting your help with my research. I have a short survey posted
on-line at www.survey.chriswogamon.com . Would you be willing to
inform your
members about the survey and provide a link to it either through your
newsletter or an email message. They will not be asked to identify
themselves by name and can submit the survey either electronically or
via
mail. If you would be willing to help with this project, please let me
know
how many members you have and how you will be sharing this with them. I
can
provide an announcement that can be put in your newsletter or sent via
email. Your help is greatly appreciated.



Lynn Wogamon
