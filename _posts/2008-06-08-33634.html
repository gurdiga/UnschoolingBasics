Hi all,
I began homeschooling my then 7 year old daughter in Sept 2007. I am a
single mom homeschooling a single child and loving it.

I made it through the first year! Honestly, even though I had some
tragedies in my life this year, we have absolutely loved our first year
hsing.

I started out wanting to unschool, freaked out and changed my mind and
now; after the first 'year' am going back to unschooling. It was my
first instinct and the more I read, the more I love.

I still feel like I don't know what I am doing most of the time, but
it's a more controlled 'out of control' feeling. ;)

Just wanted to say hello and thank everyone for all the helpful
information I have gleamed from this group.
