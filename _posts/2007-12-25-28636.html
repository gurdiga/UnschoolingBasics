Someone had been asking about a group for divorced or single parents
on this list some time ago. She got a couple of links at another list,
but I thought maybe others here could benefit from these two lists.
One is for families with "alternative" situations...divorced, single,
more than two parents, adoptive, foster, etc....
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/crazylifeunschoolers/

The other is for single unschooling parents:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/UnschoolingSingleParents/

Hope those links are helpful to some folks here. Pass them along to
anyone you think might benefit from that support.
