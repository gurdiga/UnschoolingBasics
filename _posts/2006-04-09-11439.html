Hi again. so then how much is the conference. and how much is the
hotel?

-=-=-=-

Special conference rate is $89/night---BUT that rate expires on August
6th! Even if you're on the fence and *might* come, I'd suggest at least
getting a room booked. You can always cancel the room at the last
minute. The regular hotel rates are HIGH (this is a NICE hotel!), and
there's nothing I can do about the rate after the 6th!

The conference is $350 for the whole family for Wednesday evening
through Sunday afternoon. OR $110 per adult and $75 per child. The
family deal works best if there are four or more family members coming.
Price jumps up considerably after August 10th, with no family discount.
For more info:

http://www.liveandlearnconference.org/registration.html

Dozens of funshops and speakers and just great things to do and people
to meet. It's a BUSY few days, but a lot of fun!
