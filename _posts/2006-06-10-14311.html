I thought I'd better explain the whole moderation issue, to lessen
confusion.

You don't have to post an intro. to get off moderation. We simply have
all new members set to be moderated so we can filter both SPAM and new
members that might not really be here to discuss unschooling.

It's a safety net, to keep the list on track, that's all. We don't
like heavily moderated lists, so we try to get people off moderation
as soon as possible.
