15 Aug 2009, at 2:13pm

Last chance for general registration!
Many of you have already reserved your spot at the conference  in that
case, we look forward to seeing you there! We have a few beds still
available, so we're keeping general registration open until noon on Monday.
If you're interested in attending, make sure to get in touch by that time!
We have an easy-to-follow registration form on our website where you can
register, sign up for housing and food, include a photo and message for the
conference directory, and even pre-order conference t-shirts! After Monday,
August 17th, conference registration costs will go up to the final
registration level of $30/adult, $25/child, and $100/family. Conference
T-shirts will not be available after Monday (except by extreme luck), so
pre-order yours now! Enjoy Life is just over a month away now, and the
conference is taking shape beautifully. We know, because we've talked to all
the wonderful unschooling families who've registered already; and we're
excited to meet everyone face-to-face. If you haven't checked out our
conference website, take a look at the lists of talks
<http://enjoylifeunschooling.com/things-to-do-and-hear/talks/> , chats
<http://enjoylifeunschooling.com/things-to-do-and-hear/chats/> , and
funshops <http://enjoylifeunschooling.com/things-to-do-and-hear/funshops/> 
there should be something for everyone, from unschooling families with teens
and older children to families new to the unschooling lifestyle; from those
worrying about nuts-and-bolts issues of unschooling to people just looking
to meet and spend time with like-minded parents and children.
