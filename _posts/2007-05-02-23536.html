Issue 6 of *Connections: ezine of unschooling and mindful parenting* is
now available online, offering some really terrific articles that we
hope will inspire and interest: http://connections.organiclearning.org

Joanne Greco has a feature article in this month's issue, titled
"Unschooling As a Tool for Healing" where she discusses pulling her
children out of school to promote family bonding and healing from past
trauma from the foster system. Dayna Martin explores the connections
between Heavy Metal music and unschooling as she muses on the kind of
parent she strives to be in the other feature article, and Imagination
Station this month focuses on Brik Wars in the Rossing family. Never
heard of Brik Wars? Check it out--a whole new way to play with legos!

Our regular columnists offer thought-provoking discussions in this
edition, ranging from learning despite "Education" to music festivals to
mantras and dreams. Also in this issue is Rue Kream's last Ask Rue
column--we'll miss you Rue!

*Connections* is pleased to offer such an inspiring line up of articles
for Issue 6. Thank you all for your support and enjoy!

Sorry for the confusion. I just changed the format of the index page to
make the "Subscribe" page more obviously accessible. Now folks who visit
the main page will see in the left sidebar under "Log In" the option to
either view a sample issue or to go immediately to the subscribe page. I
also changed the sidebar on the sample issue as well to try to make the
subscription page more obvious.

Anyone may subscribe by going to
http://connections.organiclearning.org/subscribe

I'd like also to remind folks that usernames and passwords are *case
sensitive*, so log-in trouble 9.9 times out of 10 is solved by checking
the caps; only twice has it been a browser issue.

There have also been a few instances of the username and password email
being dumped directly in people's spam folder. If you haven't heard back
after subcribing, *please* check your spam folder.
