I should also add that Peabody isn't IN Boston. It's a suburb, and
it's far enough away from Boston that you really don't have to go
anywhere near it, if you're nervous about driving in an unfamiliar
city. When we drive down to DC to visit my family, it takes us about
4 hrs to get from the Lowell area (about 1 hour west & north of
Boston) to just over the Tappan Zee. If you want our driving route, I
would be happy to give it to you off-list (we tend to avoid I-95).
