I'm in the very beginning stages of planning a NW Unschooling
Conference for this Spring, to take place in April or May 2007. If I
can pull it off <g>, it will have a program similar to the Live and
Learn Conference with some speakers, discussion groups, raffles,
funshops, a talent show, etc. Maybe even a few surprises of our own.

Before I get too far into the planning, I need to know how much
interest there is in having a NW conference of this type.
Specifically, I'm trying to get a feel for about how many might come
and how big a hotel we would need.

It would be held in Oregon, most likely Corvallis but definitely
somewhere from Eugene to Portland. I don't yet know how long we'd make
it or how much it would cost. That will all depend on how many are
interested.

So, if you'd like a NW conference, now's the time to speak up. Let me
know if you'd be likely to come and if you'd stay at the hotel.

Thanks,
Mary
