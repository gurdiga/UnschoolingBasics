Hi all,

I thought you might be interested in learning more about our cohousing
community in Western Middlesex County. Cohousing is a type of
neighborhood designed and managed by the people who live there. It
combines privacy and community in a clustered, pedestrian-oriented
neighborhood.


You own your own home, as well as a share of the common facilities. The
common building may include a large dining room, kitchen, lounges,
meeting rooms, children's space, library and workshops. Learn more at
our web site! -- http://www.mosaic-commons.org

This particular group has several unschooling families, although it is
open to folks of all educational philosphies.

Please join us to learn about cohousing, and how we're building it.

When: 1:00 on the 3rd Sunday of the month (Date)

Where: Assabett Valley Family Networks, (Boys and Girls Club) Marlboro, MA

For information, directions, a ride from the MBTA or to arrange
childcare, call 508-453-0566 or email info@....

"People who choose cohousing come from a wide range of cultural, ethnic,
economic and professional backgrounds. It is a goal of cohousing
communities that their residents encompass various ages and stages of
life. In much the same way as an old fashioned village, the community
embraces its residents as they move through life." - The Cohousing
Network Feel free to forward this message around.
