Julian Baptista will be performing a concert, followed by a party, for his
"commencement" from unschooling in Salem, Massachusetts on Saturday, May 19th.
He will be accompanied for parts of his performance by friends and teachers
who have influenced him as a musician, including his band, Geist. Julian
performs a wide variety of types of music, and it will include some original
pieces.

He'd like to invite unschoolers and others who are interested to attend. (If
you have a reluctant unschooling parent or relative or kid, this might be a
nice way to help them understand what unschooling is all about.)

If you'd like an invitation with details, please e-mail me off-list.
