Are you interested in travelling to meet other unschoolers? Want to know
where unschooling conferences are happening around the country?

The Unschooling Circuit Riders yahoo group is a low traffic on topic list -
twice monthly listings of all upcoming unschooling focused conferences in North
America with links to the conference websites, and significant updates and
announcements from conference organizers. Also available at the yahoogroups site
is a map of conference locations, upcoming calendar, and links to conference
information.

To join the group send a blank email to
UnschoolingCircuitRiders-subscribe@yahoogroups.com 

To check out the map 
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/unschoolingcircuitriders/app/placesmap/view/map

To join via the web visit
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/unschoolingcircuitriders
