My daughter used Game FAQs a lot:

http://www.gamefaqs.com/

In addition to walkthroughs, they often have other types of FAQs for
each game, like lists of enemies and so forth.

And if you type in "kingdom hearts strategy guide" at Amazon there
are bound to be some used guides for a few dollars. They have maps
and charts and lists galore. As a bonus to being useful for the game,
a great *real* exposure to math being put to real uses.
