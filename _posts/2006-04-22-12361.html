" He was ridiculed once when he was a young boy because
he wasted time by floating on his back on a lake. He was holding onto a
kite (Ben and his kites) and letting the kite pull him around the
lake. His mind was all a whirl on how this was happening and he
enjoyed the feeling of being pulled across the lake by just the wind's
influence on the kite. It's a great story."

I told this story at my "Unschooling a Tribe" talk last year!!
I wasn't sure if it was true, so I prefaced it with "haven't checked
into the accuracy of this" but as I told it I could see heads nodding
so several audience members knew it.:)

Very cool story.
I used it to illustrate how ALL of our children's activities are
important....how we need to trust that they're getting something
important out of even seemingly bizarre activities.
