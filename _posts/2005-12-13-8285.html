Welcome!

I call it PUP, periodic unschooling panic! I still get it and usually over
the slightest of things :)

I deal with it several ways. I have an unschooling friend that I call and
we have a good laugh about it. Or I get on these lists and read archives or
get on Joyce's list and read. Print out some "inspiration" and keep them
handy.

Perhaps the best thing is to stop what you are doing and go sit with your
kids. See them through the eyes of unconditional love, not as the "sole source
of their education". Relax and have fun. Go someplace new or watch a
video.

Oh, and Kelly would say "go to the Live and Learn Conference next year". :)
